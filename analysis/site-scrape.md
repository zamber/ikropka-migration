## Executive Summary

IKROPKA.eu is a comprehensive WordPress-based website for a landscape architecture firm based in Wrocław, Poland. The site showcases a portfolio of 130+ projects, extensive service offerings in landscape design and dendrology, and serves as both a marketing tool and client resource center.

**Key Statistics:**
- **Total Portfolio Projects:** 130+ individual project pages
- **Service Pages:** 14 detailed offer subpages
- **Main Navigation Sections:** 7 (O nas, Oferta, Portfolio, Referencje, Aktualności, Blog, Kontakt)
- **Images:** 200+ across the site
- **Technology:** WordPress with custom theme "ik", LiteSpeed server
- **Target Audience:** Private clients, architects, investors, local governments
