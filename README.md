## Main Site Structure

### Primary Navigation
1. **Homepage** - Hero, services, featured projects, testimonials
2. **O nas** (About) - Company history, team, certifications, stats
3. **Oferta** (Offer) - 14 specialized service subpages
4. **Portfolio** - 130+ filterable project case studies
5. **Referencje** (References) - Client testimonials
6. **Aktualności** (News) - Company news and updates
7. **Kontakt** (Contact) - Contact form and information

- **Dendrology** (6): Inventories, management plans, expert opinions, inspections, emergency services, protection projects
- **Design** (4): Site development, green space design, private gardens, parks
- **Other** (4): Supervision, historic objects, consulting, training
