May in the office passes in the blink of an eye. We tirelessly fight against time to meet all deadlines. We operate simultaneously in the field conducting tree inventories, as well as in design.

We are handling several large topics at once – we are closing work on the construction project for greenery for the Business Garden office complex in Wrocław, delivering the site development project for the buildings of the River Point investment for ARCHICOM, finishing the program-spatial design concept for the Youth Park in Świdnica together with an extensive tree inventory covering over 2000 trees, as well as finalizing the design of a garden layout spanning over a hectare. Furthermore, Dominika Krop-Andrzejczuk conducted a historical and natural walk through the parks of Brochów and Bieńkowice.
