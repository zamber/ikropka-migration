Tree inventory and archival query for Powstańców Śląskich Square in Wrocław


We are implementing the topic Flowers at Powstańców Śląskich Square in Wrocław. This week we conducted a greenery inventory. We also carried out an archival query.

The inventory covered the entire area of the roundabout – including the rows of oaks along the outline, mixed-species shrub groups and aged tree specimens in groups and as solitary trees. 185 trees and over 50 shrubs were inventoried.

We conducted an archival query of source materials available at the Building Archive of the City of Wrocław in Wrocław, analyzing the compositional layout of the area in question over the years.

In the green space design, the inventoried existing species as well as the historical analysis of site development will be significant guidelines for the design assumptions.
