References regarding the task related to the tree stand inspection in Wejherowo.


![We have received new references]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/ikropka_referencje-Wejherowo.jpg)

The objective of the study was to perform a tree inspection with regard to the safety of people and property. Each of the trees and shrubs was inventoried, described and assessed in terms of sanitary condition; subsequently, in the management plan, they were classified for retention, tree care or sanitary tree removal.

The study covered a part of the park area divided into five sectors, with a total area of 11.5 ha. A total of 2139 items were recorded, including 2081 trees and 58 shrubs.

Scope of the performed task:

- tree inspection for safety purposes
- dendrology management plan
- tree inventory
