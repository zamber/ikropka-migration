The dendrology inspection in the popular Urban Park A. Majkowski in Wejherowo covered an area of 11.5 ha in total, divided into five sectors. The study included 2,139 trees and shrubs. Nearly 50 different species were distinguished.


Urban Park A. Majkowski, located next to the market square of Wejherowo, is one of the most important public spaces of the city, visited daily by hundreds of residents and tourists. The park underwent revitalization in 2011.

Work on the dendrology inspection began with a field survey, which took nearly 2 weeks in total; subsequently, the collected dendrometric data, described recommendations, health conditions, etc. were transferred to a digital version. The prepared drawings and the list of inventoried trees and shrubs were summarized in the descriptive part containing tabular compilations.
