We present photographs of the completed construction project of the green roof system, greenery and mineral surfaces by IKROPKA for the Business Garden Wrocław office complex. Total area of the developed site: 4.71 ha! The project was prepared on commission from the J.S.K. Architekci studio.

The entire site is within the range of the underground garage, which required designing a complex green roof system – each type of site use required the application of a different system dedicated, for example, to green areas, pedestrian routes, or surfaces exposed to the greatest loads such as fire roads.

The designed greenery serves a representative role, reflects the modern character of the complex, and also composes chromatically with the architecture.

The [construction project](https://ikropka.eu/projekt/business-garden-wroclaw-projekt-przestrzeni-biurowej/) was developed on the basis of the tender design by RS AK and APA Wojciechowski.
