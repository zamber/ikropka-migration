We are looking for an architect / landscape architect to work at the Wrocław-based Landscape Architecture Design Studio IKROPKA.


**Requirements:**

- Availability: Mon – Fri – hybrid work system (during the pandemic), i.e. partly in the studio office, partly home – office, and field work.
- Good command of AutoCAD in the latest versions, Photoshop (or other graphic software), Office (Word, Excel).
- Freehand drawing – ability to present visions and own ideas for client meetings and internal working meetings at the studio.
- Knowledge of the basics of public space design – squares, plazas, urban gardens, etc. (no need for plant knowledge, the studio has separate specialists for that).
- Ability to prepare design documentation in the scope of site development – concept design, construction project, construction project in terms of producing independent drawings, cooperation with branch designers, project descriptions, technical specifications.
- Ability to prepare designs of small architectural forms – platforms, stairs, low walls, seating, play elements dedicated to children, etc. (in cooperation with our structural engineer).
- Basic general construction knowledge regarding familiarity with materials for small architectural elements and surfaces.
- Ability to manage a project, independently lead a site development project in public space – deadlines, division into micro-tasks, goal implementation, subcontracting work, official arrangements.
- High motivation to work in public space design, landscape architecture, openness and willingness to develop and further educate in design, technical and pro-ecological solutions characteristic of this type of space.
- Personal traits: communication skills, ability to organize own work, ability to cooperate in a team, adaptability, responsibility, loyalty, honesty, diligence, high personal culture, sense of aesthetics.

**We offer:**

- Work in a studio with an established position on the Polish landscape architecture market, with experience since 2007.
- Work on interesting commissions – recreational areas such as squares, urban plazas, external areas of housing estates, urban parks and gardens, natural, thematic playgrounds, etc.
- Cooperation in an experienced team of landscape architects.
- Workstation – computer and software
- An assistant to help with ongoing projects.
- Civil law contract
- Friendly work atmosphere

Candidates are asked to send:

- CV with photo, date of birth
- Portfolio

to email: rekrutacja@ikropka.eu

Application deadline: until the end of February 2021.
