Works related to the revitalization of Park Uśmiechu at ul. Wolbromska in Wrocław according to our project – Stage I are completed and accepted.

For PAK IKROPKA this is an exceptional investment, as it was preceded by years of work – first social work, during which we conducted social consultations with residents, and later, based on the collected data – residents' needs – developed a concept for the Wrocław Participatory Budget. On the commission of the Municipal Greenery Board in Wrocław, we prepared full design documentation regarding the site development of this area.

A new natural playground was created (for older children of the "adventure playground" type and younger ones – for playing in sand), an outdoor gym, a recreational glade with dog access, a corrected path layout with innovative wooden edging protecting the root system of trees, large-area shrub-perennial beds, lamps and a cabinet for organizing outdoor events, as well as elements of landscape furniture and site equipment (benches, bicycle racks, educational-informational boards, litter bins, decorative boulders).

We are pleased with another completed investment according to our project and at the same time thank the Municipal Greenery Board in Wrocław for the trust and cooperation.
