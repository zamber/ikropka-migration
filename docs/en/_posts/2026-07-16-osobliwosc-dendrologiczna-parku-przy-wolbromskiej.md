In the inventoried park at ul. Wolbromska we found a dendrological curiosity. This rarely encountered tree species is the panicled goldenrain tree.

The panicled goldenrain tree (*Koelreuteria paniculata*) grows in the eastern part of the study area. It is a small tree with a circumference of 38 cm, reaching a height of 6 m, in good sanitary condition. Currently in bloom!

This species originates from East Asia: China, Korea, Japan. It is distinguished by high attractiveness. The inflorescence in the form of characteristic 30-centimetre panicles in bright yellow. The fruits – pinkish inflated capsules. Also interesting foliage – compound leaves, leaflets divided in various ways, colour variation throughout the season from bright red to yellow in autumn.
