Following the completion of the revitalization project in the historic park, we are creating the as-built green space design!

The construction works of Sikorski Park have been completed; we are already after the official opening organized by the City of Świdnica with the Mayor. The park delights the residents and keeps receiving new words of appreciation, for which we warmly thank you!

Meanwhile, the City of Świdnica commissioned us to prepare the as-built project of the park. We are glad about the continuation of cooperation on a subject we had the pleasure to lead from the very beginning – from the program-spatial concept of the park revitalization, through the construction project for greenery, author supervision over the implementation, up to the as-built project.

The works on the as-built project consisted of preparing the descriptive and drawing parts for the greenery. On-site, field measurements, counts, and verification of the location and species of planted vegetation were carried out.

The project assumed tree and shrub plantings – comprehensive compensation for greenery losses in exchange for the conducted tree removals. The renewal of degraded high greenery consisted of supplementing existing groups with native species such as pedunculate oak or common beech, and adding individual tree plantings characteristic of the historic layout, i.e. common maple 'Lorbergii' or eastern hemlock.

The restoration of the low greenery layer was of significant importance, among others, due to the terrain formation. The newly planted vegetation will contribute to reducing surface water runoff, and thereby improve the stability of slopes and embankments. As a result of the plantings, a rich, multi-tier and multi-species greenery layout was created, providing favorable conditions for birds, insects, and small mammals.
