Pocket Smile Park Project in Borek

Project 636 "Pocket Smile Park at Wolbromska" has successfully passed the verification stage. Very soon we will gather all our forces to promote and collect votes in the competition; we count on the residents of the surrounding area and the whole of Wrocław!

The Pocket Smile Park can become a great place for neighborhood meetings, but also a favorite nook in the city that is always worth visiting.


In the quiet of single-family houses and tenement buildings of the Borek housing estate, partially surrounded by a wall, lies a mysterious nook. A forgotten place into which we decided to breathe new spirit.

The park area is partially surrounded by walls. We plan to turn them into a kind of playground, placing on them interactive play and learning elements as well as decorative lighting in interesting forms. The highlight of the playground is to be tree houses – a form that children can climb using ropes, via a net, and swing. We intend to diversify the terrain relief by introducing a sledding hill. We want to return part of this space to them – the children. To give them a smile – as the UN slogan from 1979 preserved on the park wall says.

The remaining part of the area has been composed as a relaxation zone and a neighborhood zone – a meeting place equipped with benches, chess tables, a wooden platform and a "neighborhood bench under a birch".

The park will not lack lighting, new paths, colorful plantings of small shrubs and perennials, and an outdoor gym. Thought has been given here to every user. The Smile Park at ul. Wolbromska, al. Hallera and ul. Racławickiej can give a smile to more than one child and adult – not only in winter!
