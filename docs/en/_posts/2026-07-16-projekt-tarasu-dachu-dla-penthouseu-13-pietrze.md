Site development, furnishing and greenery design of the terraces of a Wrocław penthouse.

IKROPKA designs the site development and greenery of two rooftop terraces with a total area of 130 m², belonging to a penthouse on the 13th floor of the building.

Exclusive furnishings, high-quality materials and distinctive greenery – these are the values guiding our initial concepts. Key design issues include creating a space that serves as an extension of the living room, suitable both for entertaining guests and for intimate relaxation.

The Clients' wishes include, among others, the creation of a grill zone, a sauna, as well as an orangery. The entire site development will be surrounded by a large amount of greenery. The style of the terraces will reference the interiors designed by interior architects in a modern and elegant character.
