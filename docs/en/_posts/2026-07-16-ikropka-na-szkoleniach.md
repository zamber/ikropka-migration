The end of the year, apart from intense ongoing work, is passing for us with training sessions and acquiring new qualifications!

Last month three of our employees participated in the Dendros training on methods of assessing the condition, vitality and statics of trees with consideration of the safety aspect, conducted by dr Edyta Rosłon-Szeryńska in Łódź. Subsequently, our team was joined by a specialist – dendrology supervision inspector Paweł Andrzejczuk, who in the training cycle "Organization of construction works in wooded areas" of the Krajowy Instytut Polityki Przestrzennej i Mieszkalnictwa obtained the certificate of Dendrology Supervision Inspector in the investment process and from now on will permanently support IKROPKA in preparing dendrology documentation and provided supervisions.

Today our new employee is participating in the two-day training of Fundacja EkoRozwoju "Why and how to protect trees? Standards and good practices", where the special guest was Dominika Krop – Andrzejczuk. As part of the training, she spoke about her experience as an activist, gained through activities for the creation of Park Uśmiechu na Borku in Wrocław.

And in a week we will participate in the Green Wrocław Symposium "Integrated design. Green and energy-efficient buildings.", organized by the Department of Sustainable Development of the Municipal Office and the Museum of Architecture in Wrocław!
