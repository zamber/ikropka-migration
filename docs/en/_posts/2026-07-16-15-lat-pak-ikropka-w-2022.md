It was a very busy year. It is unbelievable that when we sat down to summarize in January, it turned out that PAK IKROPKA had passed 15 years of activity! And although looking back we see a wealth of experience, effort and hard work, we can confidently say that it was a good time for us!

We are happy that we can be where we are, where we are entrusted with large and difficult projects in the field of landscape architecture and dendrology as well as green space inspector supervision on challenging investments. We are proud of our achievements so far, and what is more – hungry for further professional challenges.

I THANK:
– my team, who survived despite the pandemic "baby boom" 😉;
– all our regular Clients, Contractors, Investors, including offices and institutions, for their trust and loyal returns, for their belief in our capabilities, creativity and reliability;
– the wonderful architects who, in frequent and long-term cooperation, trust us and allow for partnership-based shared responsibility in creating spaces around building objects enhanced by us with greenery and technological solutions supporting it;
– my husband for his support in everyday hardships. Without you it would not have succeeded.
