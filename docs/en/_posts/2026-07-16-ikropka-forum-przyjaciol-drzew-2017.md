We are always glad to take the opportunity to expand our knowledge, broaden our horizons, and learn from the best.

Last week we participated in the two-day Friends of Trees Forum.


IKROPKA has for years designed in a tree-friendly manner; in our projects we recommend with particular care solutions most beneficial for trees. As landscape architects we know it is worthwhile. We are pleased that we could once again take part in the Forum, learn something new, and meet interesting people. It is good to exchange views and experiences, both between cities and internationally.
