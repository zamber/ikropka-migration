During the two-day conference "Współczesna Architektura Krajobrazu 2019", organized by the Landscape Architecture Association, we had the opportunity to listen to lectures by renowned European landscape architects, namely: Jo Thompson, Noel Kingsbury, Marc Weißhaar, Peter Berg. We also participated in training workshops led by the British gardener and green space designer Noel Kingsbury.

Participation in the conference and workshops is another step towards continuously broadening horizons and professional development. Contact with foreign designers brought a wealth of inspiration and knowledge on the latest trends and philosophy of green space design, and also presented current problems and ideas for their solutions.

During the training workshops, Noel Kingsbury spoke about methods of designing naturalistic flower beds, where the foundation is understanding plant biology, their functioning and capacity for natural succession. We also learned about the differences between other approaches to flower bed design, such as the compositional method of Piet Oudolf.
