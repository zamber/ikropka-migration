Once again, we had the pleasure of working for the Town and Commune Office in Strzelin. Last week, we conducted field studies as part of a dendrology expert assessment of two broad-leaved lindens.

The aforementioned lindens are part of an attractive avenue arrangement along the exit road from Strzelin. We carried out the expert assessment using specialized equipment – a sonic (acoustic) tomograph. We examined the trees at two and three heights, thereby obtaining a spatial image of the interior trunk condition. We performed visual inspection, detailed dendrometric measurements, and ultimately thoroughly assessed the sanitary condition of the trees.
