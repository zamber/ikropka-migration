We are looking for cooperation with a landscape architect who has a green concept ;)

Do you already have experience in design work and dendrology documentation? Join our PAK IKROPKA team based in Wrocław!


**We offer:**

- work on interesting commissions in public spaces on design documentation concerning the development of green areas within a multidisciplinary, experienced team,
- expansion of current experience with new challenges, continuous development,
- internal training,
- cooperation agreement based on self-employment.

**We expect:**

- design experience min. 2 years in public spaces on the execution of design documentation related to site development, landscape furniture, greenery,
- experience in dendrology documentation – inventories, dendrology management plans, protection plans, opinions, expert assessments, etc.,
- good knowledge of plants, habitats, species identification,
- fluent work in AUTO CAD 2D, basic operation of QGIS (to the extent necessary for data entry into SIP), Sketchup + PS (Blender will be an additional advantage),
- CID or INTZ qualifications will be welcome,
- surfing on social media like 🐠 (FB, Instagram etc.), text writing skills,
- high personal culture,
- reliability and meticulousness,
- fascination with the profession and willingness for self-improvement, further training,
- good communication with others.
