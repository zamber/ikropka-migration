We do not slow down the pace in our studio!
We are currently working on a construction project for greenery for an investment located in the heart of Wrocław.
FANDOM – as this is what we are talking about – will soon gain a new look.


The pavilion is part of the Wrocław Manhattan located at pl. Grunwaldzki in Wrocław. The complex, designed by the outstanding Wrocław architect – Jadwigia Grabowska-Hawrylak, was created in the late 1970s and is an indisputable icon of Wrocław modernism. Thanks to a private initiative, the revitalization of the "Fandom" pavilion is underway. Apart from the building mass itself, the owners attach great importance to the greenery surrounding the pavilion <3

In agreement with the investor, we are designing strongly naturalistic, lush perennial and grass beds, which combined with the raw facade of the building will take us straight back to the 1970s. Beyond the obvious aesthetic values, in the species selection we placed strong emphasis on the biocenotic value of the designed plants, so as to support urban biodiversity.

In addition, we ensure that the designed greenery is:
- attractive throughout the year, also outside the vegetation season;
- as easy to maintain as possible – we opt for resilient species that cope well in urban spaces;
- friendly to pollinators – we select nectariferous species, but also those that provide shelter for beneficial insects;
- biodiverse.
