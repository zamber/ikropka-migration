We prepared a dendrology management plan for the park at ul. Wolbromska in accordance with Presidential Order No. 1217/19 on the protection of trees and the development of greenery areas in Wrocław.

The park designed by us is a municipal area, and is therefore subject to local law acts. The prepared plan consists of a tree inventory and dendrological valuation, a tree management project, and a tree protection project for the construction site.

The drawing section includes the location of trees and shrubs with their numbering, crown and trunk diameters, shrub area, tree protection zones (SOD), and tree stand management.

The descriptive section includes, among others: a table with the tree inventory (Polish and Latin species names, dendrometric parameters of trees: trunk circumference, tree height, crown diameter, description of sanitary condition, tree stand management: to retain, dendrology expert assessment, care, transplanting, removal, opinions and care recommendations) as well as species composition, tree stand list, recommendations for tree protection during construction, analysis of project collisions with tree protection zones, and recommendations for avoiding collisions with the planned investment.
