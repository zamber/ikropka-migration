We have just signed a contract for the execution of a dendrology expert assessment of all trees growing on the school premises (II LO in Świdnica) with regard to their condition - in terms of potential safety hazard.


We are pleased with the cooperation undertaken – it demonstrates the growing social awareness that trees, like any other element of infrastructure, require inspection, all the more so as their condition – as living elements – is subject to change. In the field, detailed examinations will be carried out including testing with an arborist probe; each tree will be described, measured and assessed in terms of its sanitary condition, and we will also specify detailed tree care recommendations.

Tree inspections for safety purposes are a necessity and an obligation of every site manager. For a long time we have observed an increase in accidents involving trees, which prove that in urbanized areas there is a lack of constant monitoring of the sanitary condition of trees. Dry branches threatening to fall, trunks decayed at the base, improperly cared-for trees, as well as those weakened within the root ball by improperly executed earthworks may pose a fatal danger to people and property.
