title: WE SUPERVISE EXPERT ASSESSMENTS OF 10 VALUABLE TREES
lang: en

We supervise the work of Italian experts in the examination of valuable trees and advise the municipality on how to manage the greenery resource in the coming years.


The expert assessments were conducted as part of the Drogi dla Natury programme implemented in the Nowe Skalmierzyce municipality. As project animators, we designated the trees requiring detailed examination and subsequently supervised their execution.

During the field work, basic dendrometric measurements and detailed inspections of each tree were carried out, taking into account, among others, trunk defects, hazardous forks, the effects of maintenance treatments performed in previous years, and crown condition.

In several cases, the trunk wall thickness was examined using a resistograph or radar tomograph, the so-called tri-radar. Detailed care recommendations were developed for the trees, i.e. crown reduction, installation of bracing, and mounting of supports.
