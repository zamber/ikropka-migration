In May, works on the implementation of the investment "Development of public space of the WUWA area in Wrocław" were completed. The investment was carried out under author and dendrological supervision of the Supervision Inspector Dominika Krop-Andrzejczuk, IKROPKA. We present the effects of the implementation in an autumn edition.

Construction works lasted from August 2016 to May 2019. Supervision over the implementation of the IKROPKA green space design and dendrological supervision were conducted with extraordinary care. It was demanding work involving both regular, weekly visits at the construction site and numerous additional activities.

An achievement of the investment is the organization of previously neglected public spaces around the historic buildings. Thanks to the implementation of the project, the introduced site development – paths, lighting, benches, greenery, etc. – coherently reflects the modernist character of the buildings. Careful dendrological supervision produced an effect – valuable old trees were preserved and cared for, including oak avenues and the natural monument Dąb Piotra Włosta.
