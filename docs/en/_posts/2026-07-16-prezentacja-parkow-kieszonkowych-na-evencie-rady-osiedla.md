During the event of establishing a flower meadow by the Wrocław Sky Tower, a presentation of the achievements of the Rada Osiedla Powstańców Śląskich was organized.

Among the presented projects, our Pocket Parks were also presented: "Under the Silver Maple", "Perec Garden" and "Dog Hill". All of these projects were submitted in the Wrocław Citizen Budget, and then executed by our Studio in 2018, and at least one of them the City of Wrocław plans to complete still this year!

Each of the Parks was given an individually developed character, adapted to the place and needs, more about them in our portfolio:

["Under the Silver Maple"](/projekt/park-kieszonkowy-srebrzystym-klonem/) at Owsiana, Stysia Street;
["Dog Hill"](/projekt/park-kieszonkowy-psia-gorka/) at Grabskiego Street;
["Perec Garden"](/projekt/park-kieszonkowy-ogrody-pereca/) at Pereca Street opposite Pereca Square.
