Thanks to a local initiative in Przygodzice, the Alejowe Arboretum was created. The field road leading to the ponds is a beautiful corner overgrown with typical, native vegetation, and also a frequent walking route for residents as well as cycling and walking tourists. The Alejowe Arboretum project aimed to draw attention to the great importance of mid-field tree stands in the context of preserving biodiversity and maintaining ecosystem continuity.

Here, within the "Dolina Baryczy" Landscape Park, we developed an unusual and demanding project.


## Project description

The project involved conducting a tree inventory of the site's natural resources and designing new plantings selected with a view to preserving biodiversity. This task required carrying out analyses of local conditions and natural connections with the vegetation of adjacent areas. The new plantings supplemented the existing tree stands, and the species selection of the new plantings included, among others: bird cherry, white poplar, field maple, small-leaved lime, apple, pear, rose, as well as white willow, or black alder. For educational purposes, interpretive boards were designed along with their graphic and substantive development. Each planted species received its own number, which was described on one of the boards and marked in the field by a colored number tag hung on a branch. Additionally, a design for landscape furniture elements was executed.

Let us remember that in a strongly deforested agricultural landscape, mid-field tree stands create a microclimate favorable for crops. They increase the retention capacity of soils, reduce water and wind erosion. The direct proximity of mid-field tree stands and arable fields positively affects crop yields. This phenomenon extends to a distance of even 30 times the height of the tree stands!

Mid-field avenues are also a living environment for many small and large animals. From a naturalist's perspective, a single tree is like a multi-family house – it is inhabited by a number of invertebrates, birds, mammals, amphibians, reptiles, as well as plants and fungi; in this sense, an avenue is a vibrant, multi-species residential greenery!

At the environmental scale, avenue tree stands perform many important functions, including their presence contributes to improving air purity, causes a decrease in air temperature, an increase in humidity; also at the soil level, avenues have their merits in cleansing them of industrial pollutants.


## Results

The investment was completed under author supervision in 2016.
