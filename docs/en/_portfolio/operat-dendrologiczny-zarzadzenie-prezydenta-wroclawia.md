The dendrology management plan was prepared in accordance with the guidelines of Ordinance No. 1217/19 of the President of Wrocław of 28 June 2019 regarding the protection of trees and the development of green areas.


## Project Description

A tree inventory was carried out in the field, including measurements of girths, heights, crowns, and the area of trees, shrubs, and tree groups (tree girths measured at a height of 5 cm in the case of trees designated for removal). Each inventory number was described in detail and classified into one of three sanitary condition categories.

The drawing shows the inventoried trees, shrubs, and tree stands, crown diameters, trunk spread, and the Tree Protection Zones designated in accordance with the recommendations of the Ordinance.


## Solutions

A detailed analysis was made of the site development project – the project for the construction of a gas pipeline together with connections. The infrastructure was designed using trenchless technology along the entire length of the densely wooded area.

Thanks to the trenchless technology used by the designers, no tree removal was designated within the study area due to collision with the investment. Only dry or dying trees posing a threat to people and property were designated for removal. The analysis of the possibility of avoiding collisions between the project and the existing tree stand was conducted with regard to collisions of Tree Protection Zones with the planned thrust bore chambers, which are executed in open excavation.

Recommendations concerning works in the vicinity of trees, the method of protecting trees and shrubs, and the marking out of Tree Protection Zones in the field were described in detail.


## Outcomes

The use of trenchless technology made it possible to avoid tree removal caused by collision with the planned investment. Only the removal of dry and dying trees posing a threat to people and property was designated.
