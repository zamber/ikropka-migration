Dendrology supervision was carried out over the investment consisting of the execution of external electrical installations for park lighting in Park Kleciński in Wrocław.


## Investment description

As part of the supervision, our Team together with the Supervision Inspector performed an analysis of the planned execution works regarding the location of lanterns, the course of the electrical installation, the planning of thrust bores and directional drillings, as well as the possibility of extending these works, referring to the inventoried park tree stand – the spread of crowns, trunks and the root system.

At the initial stage, a site inspection was conducted with the participation of the Works Contractor, during which the scope and method of performing the works were discussed in detail.

A separate study was prepared – Tree Protection Plan, in which Tree Protection Zones TPZ and shrub protection zones were designated, and a storage location for materials during the construction period was marked in such a location that would not conflict with the existing park tree stand.

All arrangements were continuously coordinated with the Zarząd Zieleni Miejskiej in Wrocław. The supervision was carried out on the basis of Presidential Order No. 1217/19 concerning the protection of trees and the development of green areas of Wrocław, applying the recommended forms of protection. For all trees and shrubs in the immediate vicinity of the conducted works, a Tree Protection Zone was designated, which was marked out with temporary fencing. All works related to the implementation of the undertaking were carried out using the trenchless method with the application of directional drillings.


## Scope of supervision

The tasks of the Supervision during the implementation of the investment included:

- conducting branch consultations in the context of planned construction works
- conducting training for workers in the scope of properly performed works at trees
- participation in construction meetings
- supervision over the performed directional drillings
- preparing reports from arrangements from site visits
- participating in final acceptances
