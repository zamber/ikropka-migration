Compensation implemented on a plot indicated by the Municipal Greenery Board, outside the plot where tree removals were carried out. The subject of the project was compensatory planting for tree removals for the construction of the Sagittarius office building at ul. Sucha in Wrocław. Several streets within the Borek housing estate were designated for compensation.

## Project description

The project was preceded by a detailed tree inventory in the indicated locations. Street planting species were selected with regard to the existing trees. 23 ashes, 14 Swedish whitebeams and 4 lindens were planted. Great attention was paid to the distance from underground infrastructure networks when designating planting locations.

## Implementation effects

The project was developed in close cooperation with the Municipal Greenery Board and with the Office of the Municipal Monument Conservator in Wrocław. The plantings were completed.
