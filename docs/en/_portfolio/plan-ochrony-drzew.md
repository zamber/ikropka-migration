The objective of the project was to develop an action plan aimed at protecting trees on the construction site as part of the modernization of the retaining wall on Bulwarze Włostowica.

The study area is located in the strict centre of the historic Old Town, on Wyspa Piasek, within a monument protection zone. The work was therefore carried out in close cooperation with the monument conservator. The design work was preceded by a detailed tree inventory together with a tree management plan.


## Project description

Based on the redesign project of the retaining wall, a tree protection plan on the construction site and a restoration design were developed. The project included the layout of temporary pedestrian paths, construction site machinery routes, the course of fencing, as well as storage areas for construction materials. The next stage was the development of a restoration design for shrub plantings that conflicted with the construction site.


## Challenges

The study area is located in the strict centre of the historic Old Town, on Wyspa Piasek, within a monument protection zone. This required close cooperation with the monument conservator and a detailed tree inventory prior to the commencement of design work.


## Solutions

A comprehensive tree protection plan on the construction site and a restoration design were developed. Temporary pedestrian and machinery routes were laid out, along with the course of fencing and storage areas for construction materials. A restoration design was also prepared for shrub plantings removed due to conflicts with the construction site.


## Outcomes

The works were completed successfully.
