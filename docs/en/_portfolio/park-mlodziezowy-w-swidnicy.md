Park Młodzieżowy is a very historically interesting park – due to the fact that it is part of a promenade layout within the former municipal fortifications of Świdnica – with an area of 11.18 ha. This park is entered in the register of monuments of Świdnica.


## Project Description

As part of the project, a tree inventory and pre-design analyses were carried out, including: traffic analysis, dendrochronological analysis, species analysis of greenery, forest floor analysis, study document analysis (archival materials, maps, photographs, postcards, etc.), path layout concept, spatial-functional development concept of the park area, and green space concept. The project included the selection of park surfaces, landscape furniture, and species composition. The works were conducted taking into account the conservation guidelines for the park and based on the historical research carried out by a permanent collaborator – dr Iwona Bińkowska, an art historian specializing in park and garden objects, an employee of the library of the University of Wrocław, as well as on the results of analyses by the author team under the direction of mgr inż. arch. kraj. Dominika Krop-Andrzejczuk.

The concept received a positive opinion from the Monument Conservator – Dolnośląski Wojewódzki Urząd Ochrony Zabytków in Wrocław, Delegatura in Wałbrzych. The program-spatial concept included the conservation guidelines issued by DWUOZ Delegatura in Wałbrzych, as well as conclusions from the historical research and the analyses developed by the author team. For example, the boundaries of the former cemetery were particularly emphasized in the project by linear plantings in the form of low hedges. The concept assumes the reconstruction of the valuable, from a historical point of view, compositional layout of the park by clarifying the park interiors, greenery layouts, and visual axes taking into account current needs. The layout of the former kleszcze (embankments) preserved in the terrain will be clarified by organizing and emphasizing the upper contours of the embankments with ornamental shrub plantings.


## Solutions

The project provides for the preservation of the forest character of the park with the organization of existing greenery, supplementation of existing avenues, and the introduction of mid- and low-level greenery – naturalistic and ornamental shrubs, shaped hedges, and climbers. The green space design applied a species selection based on native vegetation, including bladdernut, coral honeysuckle, three-leaved spindle, small-leaved lime, and black pine.

Based on the pre-design analyses, including traffic analysis and dendrochronological analysis (age of inventoried trees), a path layout project was created, which partially reconstructs the historical course of the paths. Along the paths, niches with benches and waste bins were designed, and at the park entrances bicycle racks. The project was extended with a lighting design. An investor's cost estimate was prepared for the program-spatial concept.


## Effects

The tree inventory of the park covered over 2393 trees, which were measured, described in terms of sanitary condition, and then plotted on the dendrochronological analysis. The development phases of the park were identified and described, and trees were assigned depending on age to specific development phases of the Park Młodzieżowy in Świdnica. Thanks to the tree inventory performed, the team found in the park an extraordinary specimen of a tree rarely encountered in Poland – a variety of maple, the Cappadocian maple (Acer cappadocicum) with a circumference of as much as 360 cm – probably the second largest specimen of this species in Poland. A separate dendrology opinion was prepared for this tree with a postulate for its protection, as well as entry into the register of natural monuments.

In terms of tree stand management, among the 2393 inventoried existing trees, 148 trees were qualified for removal due to poor sanitary condition, 888 trees were qualified for care, and 1357 trees were qualified for adaptation.

The study constituted the basis for obtaining an external grant for Gmina Świdnica in the amount of 7 million PLN.
