The historic WUWA housing estate in Wrocław is a globally unique monument of modernist architecture. The experimental estate located in Dąbie has been covered by a revitalization project and activities since 2010.


## Project Description

The model-conducted investment "Development of public space of the WUWA area in Wrocław" at the implementation stage was supported by IKROPKA's dendrology supervision. The Dendrology Supervision Inspector Dominika Krop – Andrzejczuk oversees the proper course of construction works near trees, the quality of tree protection works, care works, tree transplanting, and necessary tree removal.

As part of the services provided, IKROPKA performed the following services:
- conducting specialist consultations regarding site development and greenery topics in the context of planned construction works;
- providing advice and consultations on the preparation of terms of reference concerning site development and greenery topics;
- close cooperation with the Ordering Party at the stage of conducting procurement proceedings;
- representing the Ordering Party before the Municipal Office in the scope of conducted arrangements and proceedings;
- exercising dendrology supervision over the implemented works and conducting site visits during the course of construction works.
