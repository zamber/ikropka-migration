The designed home garden is located in row housing outside the city. Due to the small size of the site, in accordance with the client's wishes, a simple and legible composition with a uniform species composition was created.


## Project Description

As part of the study, a green space design was prepared, a construction project for landscape furniture elements, a lighting design with material selection, as well as garden panel selection and their installation method were proposed. Additionally, a detailed maintenance calendar was prepared for the garden.

The colours of the landscape furniture elements were matched to the building façade and the materials used in the surfacing. In the entrance zone, simple, single-species main compositions were applied. Existing plantings were supplemented with plantings in pastel shades of pink, blue and violet. Fine light-coloured gravel was used as mulching material. Narrow strips along the driveway to the garage were supplemented with four species of ground-cover perennials.

In the rear part of the garden, concrete planters were introduced to create raised beds. On one planter with a broken, irregular shape, an L-shaped wooden bench was designed. Along the hedge in the concrete planter, bedding and large-flowered roses supplemented with lilies and low perennials were designed. The garden is adorned by three common hornbeams in the form of grafted trees with cubic-shaped trimmed crowns. The southern boundary of the plot is shaded by a tall thuja occidentalis hedge growing on the neighbour's side. Therefore, shade-loving Schizophragma hydrangeoides climbers and hostas in a concrete planter were designed. The shape of the garden is emphasised by a low boxwood edging.


## Construction

The project was completed.
