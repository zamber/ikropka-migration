The subject of the study was the preparation of a tree inventory together with an analysis of the possibilities for tree preservation in the context of the planned investment, consisting of the construction of a residential building with an underground car park and the adaptation of existing buildings for residential purposes. The study also included indications for tree stand management with regard to the sanitary condition of trees. In the field, detailed dendrometric measurements were carried out, paying particular attention to the development conditions of the root system, the condition of the trunk, and the crown. The immediate surroundings of the trees were precisely inventoried.


## Project description

Based on the site development project drawings provided by the Client, an analysis of potential conflicts with the investment was performed and guidelines were established for further proceedings during design and construction. A qualification of the tree stand was carried out with regard to the possibility of preservation, taking into account such categories as "possibility of preservation" and "possibility of preservation under special conditions". For trees designated for adaptation, care and protection recommendations were formulated, including the designation of a tree protection zone.
