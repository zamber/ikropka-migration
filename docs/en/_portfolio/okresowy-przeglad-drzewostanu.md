SAFETY IN SCHOOL AREAS

Project carried out in Wrocław, Lower Silesian Voivodeship.


## Project description

The objective of the study was to perform a tree inspection in order to ensure the safety of users of the School Complex area.

A tree management design was prepared, indicating hazardous tree stands with no prospects for improvement of sanitary condition – for removal – as well as tree stands in moderate condition requiring care works.

The works covered the entire school area including two courtyard interiors, the surroundings of sports fields and the entrance zone area.


## Results

40 tree stands in poor, hazardous condition threatening people and property were inventoried, as well as 83 tree stands in moderate condition for which necessary care treatments were designated. During field works, particular attention was paid to the occurrence of protected species.

As part of the study, a list of trees qualified for tree removal was prepared, divided into those requiring and not requiring a permit, with reference to respective articles of the Nature Protection Act.
