The training "Trees in the open landscape – user manual" in Nowe Skalmierzyce lasted two days. Topics related mainly to tree removal and good practices as well as tree diagnostics, including a field trip, enjoyed great interest.


## Training description

During the training, topics concerning the significance and functions of roadside tree plantings as well as diagnostics and natural valuation of trees were addressed.

IKROPKA, as the animator of the "Drogi dla Natury" programme, also presented the achievements to date in the Nowe Skalmierzyce commune.

Tree care using the alpine method was demonstrated in a practical manner, and methods of conducting tree inventory were shown on the example of a monumental tree.


## Effects and achievements of the programme

As part of the "Drogi dla Natury" programme in the Nowe Skalmierzyce commune, IKROPKA prepared a series of studies. All nature-valued avenues were inspected; in total, 85 avenues with a combined length of 42 435 km were inventoried.

From the inventoried tree stand, 10 trees were designated for specialist dendrology expert assessments and 10 trees for further care works using rope techniques. As part of the programme under the supervision of IKROPKA, a new avenue was also planted.

A summary of the works in the commune was the extensive document "Program zachowania zadrzewień przydrożnych gminy Nowe Skalmierzyce" (Programme for the preservation of roadside tree plantings of the Nowe Skalmierzyce commune), containing a detailed characteristics of all avenues as well as recommendations and guidance related to tree protection and the establishment of new plantings.
