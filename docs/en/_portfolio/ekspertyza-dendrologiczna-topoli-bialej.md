Dendrology expert assessment using a sonic tomograph.


## Project description

The assessment was conducted to determine the sanitary condition of the tree, and to exclude or confirm the extent of trunk decay. For this purpose, a sonic tomograph was used in the assessment, which allows determining the level and range of wood decay. Furthermore, measurements of basic tree parameters were performed in accordance with standards, along with a visual assessment of health condition, taking into account tree features that may affect stability: dimensions, location, structural characteristics or disease symptoms.

The subject tree is a white poplar (*Populus alba*) with a considerable girth of 319 cm.


## Results

The dendrology expert assessment of the tree allowed determining its actual health condition. Based on the examination results, the Client received guidelines for further proceedings regarding the tree.
