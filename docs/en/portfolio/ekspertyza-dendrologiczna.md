The subject of the study is the preparation of a dendrology expert assessment of a pedunculate oak (*Quercus robur*) in view of the investment planned by the Ordering Party. The prepared documentation aimed to assess the sanitary condition of the tree and the possibility of its preservation, as well as to issue any recommendations for further proceedings regarding it during the construction works and after the completion of the construction.


## Project description

Upon the Investor's request, we prepared pre-design studies within the boundary of the investor's plot together with its buffer zone. Due to the exceptional location of the area covered by the investment within the **Szczytnicki Natural and Landscape Complex** in Wrocław, as well as the proximity to old trees, including several natural monuments, we performed:

- a tree inventory,
- a management plan for the existing tree stand together with a tree condition assessment and an analysis of the possibility of avoiding conflicts between the existing tree stand and the planned investment — including a project for transplanting large trees, a project for tree removal of trees in poor sanitary condition and conflicting with the investment, as well as a project for tree care among trees intended for adaptation,
- a tree protection project for the building permit design phase of the investment,
- a tree transplanting plan developed in cooperation with experts in transplanting large trees,
- an expert assessment for trees bordering the planned cubature objects as well as the related technical infrastructure and necessary deep excavations.


## Result

Due to the location of the planned investment in the immediate vicinity of the **historic Park Szczytnicki** in Wrocław, areas within the historic urban layout of the Zacisze housing estate in Wrocław, recorded in the register of the Provincial Office for Monument Protection, and old trees, including natural monuments, the project constituted input for the environmental impact report. An **environmental decision** was obtained for the implementation of the investment.
