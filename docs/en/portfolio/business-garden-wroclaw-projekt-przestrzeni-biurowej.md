IKROPKA studio prepared the construction project for greenery as well as mineral surfaces and green roof systems for the Business Garden office complex in Wrocław. The project was developed in cooperation with J.S.K. Architekci, with Vastint Poland as the Investor. The design was developed on the basis of the tender design authored by RS AK and APA Wojciechowski.


## Project description

The project provides for the introduction of a green roof system on most of the area under study, both on green areas – intensive green roof, and on roads, parking lots, pedestrian routes and fire roads. The design work required constant coordination with other disciplines.


## Solutions

The designed greenery consists of single-species, large-area plantings of ornamental grasses and shrubs, as well as trees, flower meadows and various types of lawns.

Multi-stem shrubs planted in planters were designed, trees on mounds located on green roof systems, as well as in native soil, stabilization systems for trees and multi-stem shrubs, as well as irrigation-aeration systems, climbers with their systems, extensive roof. Plant material of the highest quality was designed, with large size parameters including: small-leaved lime 'Greenspire' height 6-7 m, trunk circumference 30-35 cm, silver birch 'Doorenbos' height 6-7 m, trunk circumference 35-40 cm.

An important element of the project was the issue of preparation of soil and substrates for plantings. Analyses of native soil were recommended as well as appropriate treatments to obtain the desired soil quality.

Among the applied green roof system solutions, there were 6 different systems, which are the result of different types of land use.

Mineral surfaces were used in the project for pedestrian and carriageway routes.


## Results

A technical specification was prepared for the project. The entire project was developed in a bilingual version – Polish and English.
