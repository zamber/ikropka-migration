As part of the implementation of the WBO project "Kwiaty na Rondzie – ogród miejski w centrum miasta", the IKROPKA Studio prepared the construction and construction project together with technical specifications, cost estimates and bills of quantities, two design concepts, an archive query and a tree inventory.


## Project description

The Studio also conducted administrative procedures, coordinating the project with the Monument Conservator (Municipal Monument Conservator in Wrocław) and submitting the project to the Department of Architecture and Construction of the Wrocław Municipal Office.

Thanks to the archive query conducted, historical materials were found with a drawing of a fragment of the square from 1900, presenting the composition of a garden parterre and a decorative floral arabesque.


## Design solutions

The project referenced the outline of the arabesque and also repeated its inspired shapes in the remaining fragments of the square. In accordance with the suggestion of the Municipal Monument Conservator in Wrocław, geometric borders of evergreen vegetation surrounding the designed beds were introduced. This measure will extend the attractiveness of the compositional greenery layout throughout the entire year. The project also proposed the reconstruction of group plantings of rhododendrons, referring to the historical species selection of Powstańców Śląskich square.

The main assumption of the project was the floral decoration of the space, therefore it was decided to apply multicoloured perennial beds and flower meadows, which will add decorative qualities to the Roundabout. One of the designed meadows is the "Smogówka". This mixture is characterised by anti-smog properties.
