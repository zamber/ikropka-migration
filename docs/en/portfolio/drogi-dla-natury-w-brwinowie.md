IKROPKA organized another training from the series "Trees in open landscape – user manual", this time in Brwinów. IKROPKA acted as the animator of the "Drogi dla Natury" programme in Brwinów, whose continuation was the organized training.


## Training description

During the first day of the training, participants learned methods of natural tree inventory. They found out what to pay attention to when assessing the natural value of a tree, and got acquainted with the diversity of animal, fungus and lichen species associated with trees. Participants were also trained in proper tree care.

The second day focused mainly on issues of investments and management around trees. Special attention was devoted to the issue of how to protect trees during investment processes so as not to damage the tree and its root system, and moreover – what to pay attention to during plantings. A presentation on the legal aspects of issuing tree removal decisions aroused great interest.


## Effects and results

IKROPKA presented the results of a series of studies carried out under the "Drogi dla Natury" programme in the Brwinów commune. A review of all naturally valuable avenues was conducted. In total, 93 avenues with a total length of 37 580 km were inventoried.

From the inventoried tree stand, 10 trees were designated for specialist dendrology expert assessments and 10 trees for further care works using rope techniques. As part of the programme under the supervision of IKROPKA, a new avenue was also planted.

A summary of the works in the commune was an extensive document "Program zachowania zadrzewień przydrożnych gminy Brwinów" (Programme for the preservation of roadside tree plantings of the Brwinów commune), containing a detailed characteristics of all avenues and recommendations related to tree protection and the conduct of new plantings.
