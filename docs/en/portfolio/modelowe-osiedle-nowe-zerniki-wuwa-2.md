Nowe Żerniki (WUWA 2) is a residential estate designed in line with the idea of sustainable development, adapted to the contemporary needs of modern society. Within its area there are education and culture centres, retail and service points, as well as green areas integrating the whole and facilitating the building of good social relations.


## Project description

The design concept and green space design for the new estate envisage the creation of a hilly grassy space, an avenue of large trees (London plane) together with a free arrangement of recreational sets of landscape furniture consisting of planters, seats, tables, litter bins, and the greening of the market hall roof. Plants in planters and pots will be planted with a colour gradation – from green on the lawn side to shades of yellow, orange and red under the roof. The roofing above the market square is planned as an extensive green roof with sedum growth in the form of a ready-to-lay mat. The structural layout above the roof is covered with load-bearing ropes and a steel mesh forming a trellis for the growth of climbers planted on the roof in containers.


## Project status

The project is in progress.
