The subject of the commission was to prepare a dendrology management plan and a compensatory planting design for an area designated for the construction of industrial buildings. The inventory was conducted in accordance with applicable regulations, pursuant to the Nature Protection Act of 16 April 2004 (Journal of Laws 2004 No. 92, item 880). The tree stand in question was inventoried, dendrometric measurements were taken, described, and assessed in terms of sanitary condition.

It was verified whether a binding Local Spatial Development Plan is in force for the area in question.


## Project description

The inventoried tree stand was plotted on the drawing of the site development project, on the basis of which tree stand management was carried out in the context of removals due to collision with the investment.

Subsequently, the required number of compensatory plantings was consulted with the Urząd Gminy Żórawina. In accordance with the guidelines, a 1:1 ratio of removed to planted specimens was adopted. For the replacement planting design, it was decided to use tall trees such as Fraxinus excelsior and Ulmus laevis, which were planted in a row along the boundary of the plot running adjacent to the road. The row was designed in an AABAAB arrangement.


## Results and recommendations

For the designed vegetation, specifications of plant material and conditions necessary for proper execution were prepared, along with care recommendations. The planted trees will be covered by a 3-year care period.
