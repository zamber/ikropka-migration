A dendrology opinion was prepared to document the sanitary condition of trees growing in the immediate vicinity of the commencing Marina III investment. The investment was to involve the construction of a residential–commercial building together with an underground car park and site development of the buildings.


## Description of the Study

As part of the study, detailed dendrological inspections of the trees were carried out. Basic dendrometric measurements were performed and the health condition was described in detail, thoroughly diagnosing the condition of the root system, trunk and crown. Particular attention was paid to the immediate surroundings of the trees (location on an island). The study was supplemented with photographic documentation.


## Results and Recommendations

The sanitary condition of the trees was assessed as good, posing no threat to the surroundings. In order to protect the trees and preserve their good condition and vitality during the planned construction and after its completion, it was recommended to prepare a comprehensive tree protection plan and to subject the investment to dendrological supervision.
