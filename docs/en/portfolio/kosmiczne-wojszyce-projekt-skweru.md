Kosmiczne Wojszyce was created by inspiration from the name of the street on which the square is located – ulica Kosmiczna. Of the two design concepts, it was the cosmic vision that was selected both by the Project Leader and the Client.

The site development project together with the paving design, details in the form of landscape furniture, green space design and graphic design of the information board was prepared on commission from Zarząd Zieleni Miejskiej in Wrocław and is being implemented under the Wrocławskiego Budżetu Obywatelskiego 2018.

In Kosmiczne Wojszyce there are numerous cosmic references. The entire composition has a spiral layout referring to the Milky Way. In the central part of the square, a plaza with a surface that glows at night, in the shape of a galaxy, was placed. An additional advantage of the project is the designed <strong>rain garden</strong>.


## Project description

The central cosmic element of the site development is a plaza named "Galaxy", its surface contains a pigment <strong>that glows at night</strong>! Likewise at night, the constellations of the Great and Little Dipper arranged from stones and the Pillars of Creation – robinia logs painted with special paint – will light up!

In order to collect rainwater from the designed plaza, a rain garden was planned to be built right next to it. The depression will be filled with appropriate layers of aggregate and soil substrate, and finally planted with vegetation and lined with stones. Thanks to this solution, rainwater will not form puddles, nor will it flow into the storm sewer, but will be diverted for beneficial use by plants.

To make Kosmiczne Wojszyce even more attractive for families with children, a two-section <strong>barefoot path</strong> and a <strong>living willow tunnel</strong> were designed, as well as two mounds and further elements made of wooden posts and robinia logs. The square will also be equipped with waste bins and bicycle racks.

For Kosmiczne Wojszyce we designed an <em>educational information board</em> that tells about space in an interesting and simple way. The cosmic character of the site development will be unified by naturalistic elements of landscape furniture and materials. Meanwhile, the vegetation will emphasize the contrast of the cosmic landscape. Purple-yellow compositions of perennials and shrubs were used. Despite the small space, the project succeeded in incorporating tree plantings of several species.

The design work included: site visit, tree inventory, pre-design analyses, development of two concepts, consultations with the Project Leader and the Client, execution of the final concept being a synthesis of selected solutions, execution of the building project and administrative work related to the procedure at the Department of Architecture and Construction, execution of the construction project together with technical specifications and cost estimates.
