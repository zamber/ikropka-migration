The design assumption was to create a representative, modern, and pro-social place by introducing new elements of landscape furniture and an attractive composition of greenery in planters. An important guideline was to adapt to the existing site development of the interior – an elliptical grass square with a large fountain. Due to the underground parking located beneath the square, as well as the designated budget, it was decided to introduce tall greenery in planters.


## Project description

In the selection of elements, the aim was to break the rigid, geometric outline of the pavement and refer to the round and oval shapes of the lawn and fountain, while maintaining the modern character of the interior.

Therefore, the project used stylish benches with rounded shapes, complemented by large round planters for planting multi-stem shrubs. Landscape furniture of the highest quality was applied.

The greenery was designed as multi-species, multi-level compositions in planters. The main role is played by large solitary ornamental shrubs with naturalistic, multi-stem forms. They are complemented by low groundcover plants hanging from the planters, which will give the effect of lusher greenery. Additionally, a climber was planned to cover the generator building.
