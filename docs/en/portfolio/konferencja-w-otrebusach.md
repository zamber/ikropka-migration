The conference in Otrębusy entitled "Design and implementation of infrastructure in the vicinity of trees – latest solutions and good examples" was held under the patronage of the Marshal of the Masovian Voivodeship.

IKROPKA, as coordinator, handled the organization of the entire event for Fundacja EkoRozwoju and the Brwinów Commune Office.

The meeting was addressed to road administrators, officials, designers (road, installation, architects and landscape architects) and contractors from the area of the Brwinów commune and neighbouring communes as far as Warszawa.


## Course of the conference

During the lectures by dr inż. Piotr Tyszko-Chmielowiec, Dominika Krop-Andrzejczuk, Adam Rączkowski and dr inż. Marzena Suchocka, the participants had the opportunity to learn about the latest methods of carrying out investments in the vicinity of trees – from the design stage, through the organization of works on the construction site, to the implementation of the investment.

The meeting was exceptional due to the nature of the presented case study – the case of the redevelopment of ul. Pszczelińska in Otrębusy (Brwinów commune). Mayor Arkadiusz Kosiński and a local activist and road designer spoke about this model cooperation of residents, officials, specialists, Fundacja EkoRozwoju and the designer, thanks to which it was possible to plan the modernisation of the road while preserving many trees in the old, valuable lime-oak avenue.

As part of the conference, Dominika Krop-Andrzejczuk and Aleksandra Gierko also presented the results of the 'Drogi dla Natury' programme carried out by IKROPKA in the Brwinów commune.

The conference was summarised by a discussion on difficult conflicts in the investment process and their solutions.
