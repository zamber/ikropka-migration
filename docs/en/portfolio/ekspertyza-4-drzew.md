The expert assessment was prepared to examine the sanitary condition of four trees prior to the commencement of the hospital construction investment. The document specified recommendations concerning their protection in relation to the planned investment, including measures for safeguarding during the construction period and following its completion.


## Project Description

In the process of preparing the study, the site development project in the vicinity of the trees was analyzed. This enabled the issuance of guidelines for the detailing or modification of the project and further proceedings regarding the trees.


## Solutions

Among the proposed alternative construction solutions were, inter alia: the execution of a ramp walkway with a suspended structure around the tree, and the laying of underground networks using a trenchless method, so-called pipe thrusting.
