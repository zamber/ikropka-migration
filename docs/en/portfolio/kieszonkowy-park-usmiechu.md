We engaged socially in this year's edition of the Wrocław Participatory Budget – we developed the design concept and functional program of a park in Wrocław in Borek. Thanks to this project, one of the neglected backyards is to be transformed into a pocket Park Uśmiechu.

Thanks to the planned activities, the green enclave in the vicinity of the busy aleja Hallera, hidden from noise behind the facade of street-side residential buildings, will gain new life – it will become Park Uśmiechu. This place is meant to connect generations – there will be something here for adults, children, as well as seniors.

## Project description

The name of the project derives from a partially painted-over today mural with the inscription "Dzieciom uśmiech – światu pokój", which – as we established – was created in 1979 on the occasion of the International Year of the Child. An undoubted advantage of Park Uśmiechu is the presence of beautiful old-growth trees, whose qualities will be emphasized by new plantings of low greenery – colorful shrubs and grasses. Complementing the greenery is only a small part of the changes awaiting this quiet corner!

The site development of the park results from the creation of several zones for its users: cognitive, play, relaxation, physical activity (outdoor gym) and a neighborhood meeting place. To enhance the terrain relief, we planned to mound a small sledding hill, thanks to which the place will also encourage winter excursions.

The utilization of the walls surrounding the square was also designed. Interactive installations, educational boards and lighting elements are to be mounted on them. They will serve both play and environmental education. The greatest attraction is to be individually designed tree houses placed in trees or on special posts in their vicinity. The entire park will be illuminated, equipped with benches and litter bins. The project also provides for the modernization of path surfaces, installation of hammocks, play equipment and bird drinkers.

We want Park Uśmiechu to become a model example of site development of a neglected green area and an inspiration for other inhabitants of Wrocław, who have similar forgotten squares and green plots within easy reach.
