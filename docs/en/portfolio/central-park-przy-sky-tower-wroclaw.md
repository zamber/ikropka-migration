Over the years, the area between the Wrocław Poltegor and the Hotel Wrocław was the subject of successive concepts for the so-called "Southern Centre". It remained, however, a green area with spontaneously grown low and high vegetation, frequented by local residents.

At the time of the construction of the Sky Tower skyscraper in place of Poltegor, the southern part of the square was adapted into a construction site and subsequently a parking lot. In the following years, the construction of a multi-family building also began in the north-western part of the plot.

## Project Description

Meanwhile, this area has great potential as a large green area in the city centre. Such an observation became the impetus for the creation of the concept of the Wrocław "Central Park" – located in close proximity to the Market Square, in a large residential estate, conveniently connected with other parts of the city. The area is situated along the busy Powstańców Śląskich street, which constitutes the main north-south arterial road of the city. It is located halfway between Kościuszki Square and Powstańców Śląskich Square and could form an interesting counterweight to the large-scale skyscraper.

The study of the main pedestrian movement routes and the location of destination points, such as stops, allowed for the development of a communication layout connected with a walking loop around the entire park. The assumption was to diversify the terrain – hence numerous hills and the elevation of the ground from the side of the skyscraper, which additionally aims to soften the slender form of the building. The elevation also serves as an amphitheatre with seating and a viewpoint, creating new perspectives of the surroundings. Hills also appear near the newly built residential building, in the zone for playing with four-legged companions and the outdoor gym, as well as by the children's playground, where the raised terrain above the gastronomic building simultaneously forms a sledding hill. From the side of the Hotel Wrocław and the Sky Tower, representative entrance zones were created. The entire park is surrounded by a belt of high trees, which are to serve as an acoustic barrier protecting against the noise of the busy street. The complement is formed by banded perennial compositions with a predominance of ornamental grasses. In the central part there is a recreational glade with a characteristic loop serving decorative and recreational functions. Part of the loop is the aforementioned hill with a restaurant function, which can also serve for play. One of the parts also consists of a board-riding track elevated above ground level. The remaining fragment is formed from plantings of ornamental grasses. The whole will be visible from the windows of the buildings surrounding the park. Moreover, inside the loop there is a playground for the youngest.

The park constitutes an attractive green area in the centre of a large city. The proximity of a large number of multi-family buildings means that there are many potential users in the vicinity. The diversity of functions constitutes its additional advantages.
