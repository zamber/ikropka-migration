The scope of the study included the preparation of a dendrology expert assessment using specialized equipment by the elasto – inclino method (load test) of a tree of the species small-leaved lime (*Tilia cordata*) together with the development of the test results. The subject tree required determination of its actual condition, trunk strength and stability in the soil, as well as defining the level of safety in the vicinity of the tree.


## Description of the Study

A load test of the tree was carried out, as well as a simulation assuming crown reduction of the tree.

The prepared dendrology expert assessment contains a description of the research methodology, a description of the tested tree, results of measurements and tests in descriptive and graphical form, photographic documentation, and recommendations formulated on the basis of the tests.


## Results

The conducted load test allowed for a professional determination of the tree's condition and the issuance of recommendations aimed at ensuring the safety of people and property in its vicinity.
