Within the Podwarszawskie Trójmiasto Ogrodów area, covering the municipalities of Brwinów, Milanówek and Podkowa Leśna, a tree inventory with care recommendations was carried out for all natural monuments and monumental avenues. The total area of the study amounted to 9273 ha! The area was surveyed by vehicle in search of natural monuments.

## Project description

The documentation was prepared as part of the first stage of the task entitled: "Development of a strategy for the development of green areas in the Podwarszawskie Trójmiasto Ogrodów area together with a natural resources inventory and a concept for new plantings".

The objective of the inventory conducted was to collect basic data and determine the health condition of monumental trees, including morphological features that may affect tree vitality. An important element noted during the description of individual units was also the surroundings of each monument. The subsequent step was to formulate care recommendations aimed at protecting both the tree itself and its surroundings against the potential consequences of past neglect. Individual monuments were described separately, while groups of avenue trees were described separately as well.

## Inventory results

In the Podwarszawskie Trójmiasto Ogrodów area there are a total of 250 trees – natural monuments growing individually or in groups, as well as 16 monumental avenues and tree rows, in which 920 trees grow together.
