##

Founded in 2007, the Landscape Architecture Studio IKROPKA has over the years gained a reputation as a professional, reliable and punctual contractor. Its services are addressed both to private individuals and to architects, investors and local governments. The client can count on professional service and expert consulting. Every project will be executed with respect for the natural environment.

{% include feature_row %}

## Three pillars of activity

### Design

IKROPKA creates designs tailored to the needs of Clients. It can also boast extensive experience in working in spaces under conservator protection.

### Dendrology

The team dealing with dendrology studies prepares tree inventories, dendrology management plans, and in cases requiring greater detail, opinions and dendrology expert assessments preceded by tree examinations.

### Supervision

IKROPKA has for years provided dendrology supervision services – Green Areas Supervision Inspector and Dendrology Supervision Inspector in the Investment Process.

## Holistic approach

The Landscape Architecture Studio IKROPKA assists its Clients in formal and legal matters related to obtaining necessary permits, including those for tree removal. Based on applicable legal grounds, in the case of Wrocław – Ordinance No. 1217/19 of the President of Wrocław of 28 June 2019 on the protection of trees and development of Wrocław's green areas – we prepare tree data for introduction into the Wrocław Spatial Information System (SIP). We attach great importance to tree protection in the investment process by preparing studies such as tree protection project on the construction site. We support property managers and local government units, municipalities (including educational institutions) by performing tree stand inspections for safety of people and property. We also conduct training devoted to dendrological issues at every stage of investment implementation. We invite you to cooperate!
