## Park and Recreational Area Design

We design **urban parks, recreational areas and rest zones** - comprehensive development of large green spaces with accompanying infrastructure.

### Design Scope

- **Urban Parks** - residential, district, landscape parks
- **Recreational Areas** - activity zones, walking/cycling paths
- **Playgrounds** - zones for children of different age groups
- **Adventure Parks** - obstacle courses, rope equipment
- **Sensory Parks** - spaces of touch, smell, sound
- **Community Gardens** - spaces for shared use

### Project Elements

**Infrastructure**
- Path and alley layout
- Landscape furniture (benches, bins, racks)
- Lighting
- Water elements (fountains, ponds, streams)

**Vegetation**
- Tree and shrub plantings
- Flower beds and flowering meadows
- Lawn areas
- Rock garden plants

**Functions**
- Activity zones (sports, recreation)
- Rest zones (picnics, relaxation)
- Communication routes
- Accessibility for people with disabilities

### Design Process

1. **Site Analysis** - conditions, existing trees
2. **Public Consultations** - residents' needs
3. **Concept Design** - 3D visualizations, variants
4. **Technical Design** - construction drawings, specifications
5. **Cost Estimate** and schedule
6. **Author Supervision** during implementation
