Founded in 2007, the Landscape Architecture Studio IKROPKA has over the years gained a reputation as a professional, reliable and punctual contractor. Its services are addressed both to private individuals and to architects, investors and local governments. The client can count on professional service and expert consulting. Every project will be executed with respect for the natural environment.


## Three pillars of activity

The IKROPKA studio brings together specialists from various fields. Among others, landscape architects and dendrologists work here. Therefore, the company's activity focuses on three pillars: design, dendrology and supervision.


## Holistic approach

The Landscape Architecture Studio IKROPKA assists its Clients in formal and legal procedures related to obtaining the necessary permits, including those for tree removal. Based on applicable legal grounds, in the case of Wrocław – Resolution No. 1217/19 of the President of Wrocław of 28 June 2019 concerning the protection of trees and the development of Wrocław's green areas – we develop tree data for entry into the Wrocław Spatial Information System (SIP). We attach great importance to the protection of trees in the investment process by preparing studies such as a tree protection project on the construction site. We support property managers as well as local government units, municipalities (including educational institutions) by performing tree stand inspections with regard to the safety of people and property. We also conduct training devoted to dendrological issues at every stage of investment implementation. We invite you to cooperate!
