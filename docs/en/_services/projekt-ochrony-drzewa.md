## Tree Protection Plan on Construction Site

We offer **tree protection plans** against damage during construction works - in accordance with **Art. 83 par. 3 of the Construction Law**.

### When Is It Needed?

Tree protection plan is **legally required** when:
- Construction/expansion/reconstruction takes place in the impact zone of trees
- Trees are located on the construction site or directly at the boundary
- Planned works may damage the root system or crown

### What Does the Plan Include?

- **Tree inventory** of construction site
- **Layout map** of trees relative to planned investment
- **Protection zones** for each tree (crown + root system)
- **Fencing design** and safeguards
- **Maintenance recommendations** during construction
- **Work instructions** near trees
- **Dendrological supervision** - scope and deadlines

### Procedure

1. **Site visit** + tree measurements
2. **Construction project analysis** (plans, cross-sections)
3. **Protection plan development**
4. **Agreement with construction supervisor**
5. **Supervision during construction** (optional)

### Legal Basis

- **Art. 83 par. 3 of the Construction Law** (Journal of Laws 2021 item 2351)
- **Nature Protection Act** (Journal of Laws 2023 item 1336)
