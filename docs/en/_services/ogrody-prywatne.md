## Garden Design

**We design gardens:** home gardens, residential gardens, [historicizing (historic areas)](https://ikropka.eu/oferta/obiekty-zabytkowe/).

When designing gardens, we meet the expectations of the Client. We adapt the garden to the needs of users, the building mass, interior design and, of course, habitat conditions. Most important for us is to design for the Owners their dream, unique garden.

We select greenery according to habitat conditions, using e.g. plants adapted to difficult urban conditions. We advocate pro-nature solutions in every garden. When designing, we pay special attention to plastic and natural values. We strive to achieve maximum attractiveness effects throughout the entire year. We combine vegetation in terms of color, creating scent and height combinations, using multi-layering in composition and caring for their usefulness to the environment. We base on broad knowledge of garden art, styles and trends.

We readily use stone in the garden. We cooperate with Garden Stones company importing garden stones from around the world.

Your garden we will adapt to you and your family.

We also deal with the design of [historic gardens and parks](https://ikropka.eu/oferta/obiekty-zabytkowe/) under conservator protection, entered in the register or inventory of monuments. We have great experience in this field and undertake subsequent challenges with passion. We are able to conduct the entire process of consultations and official agreements necessary to commence construction works. We prepare professional historical queries carried out by an art historian, specialist in park and garden ensembles. Designs of historicizing gardens in historic areas are based on the results of conducted historical analyses, conservator conclusions and adapted to the architectural style of buildings and greenery layouts in historic ensembles.
