## About training

The qualifications and experience gathered over the years of IKROPKA's activity represent a unique spectrum of knowledge that we gladly share, as we believe that disseminating good practices develops the industry and its standards.

For everyone, we are able to prepare an individual training offer thematically directed to specific recipients: officials, designers (road, installation, architects, landscape architects), contractors, investors, staff responsible for proper tree protection, etc.

We offer conducting training in the scope of dendrology, design, design around trees using the latest construction technologies, and the significance of tree stands in the city and in the landscape.

Example training topics:
• tree stand management at the design stage (Tree Stand Management Project);
• safe design in the vicinity of trees (including Tree Protection Project);
• conducting investments in the vicinity of trees at various investment stages: planning, design, construction, post-construction monitoring;
• proper conduct of the formal and legal process regarding obtaining agreements and decisions;
• modern construction technologies friendly to trees.
