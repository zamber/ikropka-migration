## Consulting

![]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/2017/09/ikona-doradztwo.png)

We provide consulting to private individuals and companies at various stages: planning, design phase, construction, and post-construction.

**We support specialists in selecting appropriate [design solutions](/oferta/projekty-zagospodarowania-terenu/) or construction solutions in the vicinity of trees. If you have doubts regarding the correctness of selected solutions or performed works, contact us. The choice of appropriate technologies enables the realization of highest-quality investments, safe for their users.**

We help optimize **costs** for Investors already at the stage of plot purchase in the context of existing greenery.

We also advise on identifying valuable trees worthy of special protection during the investment process. For such trees, it is worth preparing a [Tree Stand Protection Project](/oferta/projekt-ochrony-drzewa/), as their damage or destruction entails enormous costs for the Investor.

**We assess [damage to greenery](/oferta/dendrolog-opinie-ekspertyzy/) and issue recommendations regarding its restoration.**

We also provide consulting services in the preparation of public procurement, including defining requirements for the Contractor and the detailed description of the subject of the contract.

We can comprehensively manage complicated administrative procedures concerning permits and decisions – also as the Ordering Party's proxy – and coordinate the work of various specialists.

*If the quality and correctness of the ongoing investment process, design process, or construction process are questionable and the need for consulting arises, with passion and commitment we will undertake the assessment of the situation and formulate appropriate guidelines for further proceedings.*
