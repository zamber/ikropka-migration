A tree inventory is an important document at the investment planning stage and a fundamental tool for effective tree stand management. We invite you to cooperate!


## Tree Inventories

We perform detailed and comprehensive tree inventories (greenery inventory) for the purpose of submitting tree removal applications, investment planning, design, construction, valuation of removals (preliminary compensation fees), tree stand management, resource valorization, preparation of replacement planting design, tree inventory, etc.

Our specialization is dendrology, i.e. a branch of botany dealing with shrubs and trees in public places, palace gardens, historic greenery and other public places filled with greenery. We prepare our studies in accordance with current legal regulations and the latest knowledge, thanks to which the Ordering Party receives complete documentation on the basis of which accurate tree inventory, tree stand management, elimination of trees threatening the safety of people and property, and optimization of the costs of any removal are possible.

Greenery inventory and tree inventory consist of two parts: descriptive and graphic. The descriptive part contains the designation of the species name of trees and shrubs in Polish and Latin, dendrometric measurements, assessment of sanitary condition and description of trees and shrubs in the context of the surroundings. The graphic part prepared in AutoCAD or GIS software presents the location of trees and shrubs on a map together with numerical designation corresponding to the tabular summary.

We prepare studies in AutoCAD or GIS software.

Additionally, a tree inventory may include a tree stand management plan, photographic documentation, determination of tree stand age (based on the method of prof. L. Majdecki), dendrochronological analyses, a table with a preliminary of fees for possible removal of trees and shrubs, a list of trees to be removed without charging fees and those qualifying for transplanting.

On behalf of the Investor – based on a power of attorney – we can also obtain an administrative decision for the removal or transplanting of trees and shrubs. We also offer preparation of an application for tree and shrub removal or preparation of its sketch.

Moreover, we also perform tree inventories together with a tree stand management plan – dendrology management plans, providing knowledge about the care treatments needed or the tree stand colliding with the design. We offer tree stand inspections for safety, in which we identify threatening trees and describe the necessary, priority treatments removing the danger around trees. We also have the knowledge and experience in preparing opinions and expert assessments, in which we thoroughly examine greenery, potentially dangerous or valuable trees. If, on the other hand, there is a sudden need for express intervention regarding e.g. a tree on a construction site, we recommend using our emergency dendrology service!

In the case of preparing a tree stand management plan as part of a dendrology management plan for trees growing in historic areas, pursuant to Art. 37a of the Act of 23 July 2003 on monument protection and care of monuments (Journal of Laws 2022.0.840), the works and documentation may be carried out by a person who "has completed second-cycle studies or uniform master's studies, in the field of conservation and restoration of works of art or monument conservation and who, after starting second-cycle studies or after passing the sixth semester of uniform master's studies, for at least 9 months participated in conservation works, restoration works or conservation research, conducted for monuments entered in the register, the inventory of a museum being a cultural institution or classified into one of the categories referred to in Art. 14a List of Heritage Treasures sec. 2." These requirements are met, among others, by Pracownia Architektury Krajobrazu IKROPKA Dominika Krop – Andrzejczuk, operating throughout Poland.
