Pocket Parks – roadside greenery enclaves – a trend or a necessity?


IKROPKA was invited to moderate one of the panels of the 6th Roadside Greenery Conference organized in September by Dendros.

Dominika Krop-Andrzejczuk will speak about Pocket Parks and the Studio's experiences with them. Among the presented examples of roadside greenery, one will be able to learn about an interesting case study of the "Pod srebrzystym klonem" Pocket Park at ul. Owsianej — the story of a roadside "wild" parking lot under trees, which will be transformed into a park. The design provides for the preservation of trees, thanks to the design of paths using a special ramp pavement technology.
