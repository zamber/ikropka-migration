We are pleased to announce that an article concerning Park Uśmiechu and its remarkable victory in the Polski Krajobraz 2020 XXL Plebiscyte – in the public space category – has been published on Wroclife.pl. Park Uśmiechu, a square at ul. Wolbromska in Wrocław, was completed according to a design by IKROPKA on the initiative of the Municipal Greenery Board in Wrocław.

We encourage you to read the article available at the link below:
