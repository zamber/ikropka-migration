Our studio prepares dendrology management plans extended with an analysis of the possibility of avoiding conflicts with the planned investment and a tree protection project on the construction site.

For the needs of the investor, our studio prepares dendrology management plans extended with an analysis of the possibility of avoiding conflicts with the planned investment and a tree protection project on the construction site.

This study – in addition to basic information on species composition, tree parameters and their sanitary condition, or stand management – contains information on whether and how conflicts with the stand on the construction site can be avoided. This analysis is carried out on the basis of the current site development project, tree inventory and the designation of the tree protection zone (TPZ). The tree protection project on the construction site includes guidelines on how to proceed in the case of works within the range of the tree protection zone (TPZ), including the method of securing trees (trunks, crowns) and shrubs, execution of a temporary road for vehicles or pedestrians, implementation of thrust bores, recommendations regarding earthworks and works in tree crowns.
