We are pleased to announce that the naturalistic urban park project at ul. Idzikowskiego by PAK IKROPKA received a distinction in the competition for the best green infrastructure project in the public space category during the World Green Infrastructure Congress (WGIC)!


![PAK IKROPKA awarded during WGIC 2023!]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/news/wgic.png)

*Click to enlarge*

During the World Green Infrastructure Congress (World Green Infrastructure Congress – WGIC), held in Berlin in June 2023, the project by PAK IKROPKA was recognized in the competition for good practices in green infrastructure. The project concerning the site development of the park at ul. Idzikowskiego in Wrocław received a distinction in the category of best urban space project!

Our project in brief:

– supports biodiversity on multiple levels
– mimics natural mechanisms in the natural environment, where the site was a challenge – a former storage yard after buildings and a construction backup of the Wrocław Gądów residential estate
– harmonizes and integrates the fourth nature with newly introduced elements in the space
– raises the issue of unsealing concrete and its absorption by existing and designed vegetation
– raises the issue of mowing / not mowing grasses in public space and regulates it spatially
– introduces climatic biogroups, which are to constitute a resource of fruiting, edible vegetation, beneficial for animals
– supports retention of stormwater
– addresses the topic of natural succession on site – we show how in a partially controlled way nature can be allowed to act on its own
– supports adaptation to climate change
– addresses the topic of education – 19 designed boards with substantive value for residents featuring original graphics – raising public awareness is key to positive reception of nature-based projects.
