Today in Wrocław a seminar and the premiere of the publication titled "Infrastructure in harmony with trees" will take place. IKROPKA will participate in this event as one of the authors of the publication.


The publication issued by Fundacja Dom Pokoju was created in cooperation with the partner SKANSKA and substantive partners Zarząd Zieleni Miejskiej in Wrocław, Urząd Miejski Wrocławia – Departament Zrównoważonego Rozwoju and Fundacja Ekorozwoju.

"Infrastructure in harmony with trees" describes, using concrete examples from the practice of Wrocław investments, how to protect trees starting from administration through to the construction site. In the publication we will read about alternative solutions reconciling infrastructure with both existing and newly planted trees, e.g. planting trees near networks, realization of large construction investments by trees, and a ramp pavement by a tree. The publication also includes a catalogue of good practices during investments and principles of forming young trees.

IKROPKA's contribution is the article "How to realize a large investment and protect trees – from the practice of a dendrology supervision inspector", in which mgr inż. arch. kraj. Dominika Krop-Andrzejczuk describes her experience from dendrology supervision over the construction of the new seat of Izba Skarbowa in Wrocław, whose main objective was the protection of a valuable tree – a London plane with a circumference of: 475 cm, crown diameter: 22.5 × 20.0 m, height: 24.0 m. In its immediate surroundings just beyond the crown projection, a deep excavation for an 11-storey building, underground garage and garage entrance was being constructed from three sides.
