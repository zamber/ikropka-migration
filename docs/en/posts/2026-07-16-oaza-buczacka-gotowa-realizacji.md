Recently we had the pleasure of releasing from our pens the construction project for site development and greenery of Oaza Buczacka in Muchobór Wielki, Wrocław. The scope of our work covered all stages from inventory, through concepts, building permit design and administrative agreements, up to the construction project.

The square, which until now was a wasteland, will be transformed into a recreational area, a green meeting place. Within the study area, a pergola was planned, diverse benches including in the form of urban loungers, flowering plantings, as well as trees with proper girth and height parameters, etc. Furthermore, an interesting landform was designed – elevations in the form of mounds and depressions – a rain garden, which will ensure slow infiltration of water into the ground, reducing the amount of water entering the overloaded water supply network of the city.
