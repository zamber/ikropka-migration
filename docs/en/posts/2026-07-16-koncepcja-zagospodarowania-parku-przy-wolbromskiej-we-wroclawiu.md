lang: en

Design works for the implementation of the task concerning the park at ul. Wolbromska in Wrocław are already at the stage of creating the construction project. We presented two concepts to the Client, and on their basis we developed the final design concept – resulting from the established arrangements.


The study area is covered by the Local Spatial Development Plan, whose boundaries are identical with the boundaries of the monument protection zone and the boundaries of the archaeological monuments protection zone. For this reason, the design concept was consulted with the Municipal Monument Conservator in Wrocław and the Provincial Monument Protection Office in Wrocław. Relevant agreements were obtained.

The design concept assumes:

– tidying up the park area, introducing a landscape style of the layout with winding paths and lush vegetation,

– equipping the area with landscape furniture elements such as: benches, litter bins, bench swings, bicycle racks and an information board,

– creating functional zones of the park due to its use and intended target groups (entrance zones, play area for older and younger children, outdoor gym zone, dog zone, open-air event zone),

– establishing lighting within the study area, vegetation introducing the climate of a "secret garden", tiered plant compositions adapted to habitats.
