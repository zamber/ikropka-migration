Wiktor Bochenek from Architektura i Biznes spoke with Dominika Krop-Andrzejczuk about our latest most demanding and innovative in approach project of Gądowian Park, a park at ul. Idzikowskiego in Wrocław.


From the interview you will learn, among others:

- About the idea of designing based on nature #NBS, deconcretization;
- About the issues of combining third and fourth nature;
- About the design of the spontaneous process effect of "concrete being absorbed by nature";
- About designing in an area occupied by nature and the mixx system adopted by us as reconciling the interests of nature and the needs of residents;
- About how to reconcile the needs of nature with the needs of humans? To what extent will this park be used by the residents of Wrocław?
- What are the "ruderal mounds" designed by us;
- About shaping the landscape through the design of mowing height and frequency.
- About the educational value of this area;
- Why we should pay more attention to designing #EcosystemBasedDesign or #NaturalBasedSolution.
