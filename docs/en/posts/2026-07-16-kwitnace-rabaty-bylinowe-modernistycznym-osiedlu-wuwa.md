Today we attended the final acceptance of the revitalization investment of the modernist model housing estate WUWA in Wrocław!

The green space design authored by us has been completed, and the author supervision has come to an end! The investment was concluded after 3 years of revitalization works. The perennial flower beds according to our design present beautifully by the gallery building at ul. Tramwajowa (next to WUWA Cafe). The coral bells (*Heuchera ×brizoides*) have just bloomed, and together with ornamental grasses they compose perfectly with the austerity of the modernist architecture.
