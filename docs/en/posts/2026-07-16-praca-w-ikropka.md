We are looking for a landscape architect designer or design assistant to join the team of our Landscape Architecture Studio IKROPKA.


![Work at IKROPKA]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/6b205bfb-a82e-4ee1-b4b0-839c7b1420f0.jpg)

We are looking for a designer or design assistant landscape architect to join the team of our studio PAK IKROPKA on a permanent contract – work in Wrocław – in the office and in the field – for cooperation on commissions carried out by the company in the area of dendrology studies and on interesting public and private projects from the field of landscape architecture (residential greenery, squares, parks, gardens, natural playgrounds, etc.) – concept designs, construction projects, construction projects.

**Scope of cooperation:**

- Cooperation on green area projects and green space design – work in CAD, Office (Word, Excel)
- Cooperation on tree inventories, tree stand management projects, dendrology management plans – field and office work

**Requirements:**

- completed studies of at least engineering degree in landscape architecture;
- experience in tree inventories, in design work;
- software: good knowledge of CAD – working with dwg files, Word, Excel, an additional asset (not required) will be handling graphic programs (Photoshop or other – to be agreed);
- personal: good work organization, reliability, accuracy, punctuality, communication skills, loyalty, honesty;
- interest in the industry, greenery, trees, willingness for professional development – readiness for training;
- readiness for stationary work in our office in Wrocław (possibility of occasional hybrid mode – to be agreed) from Monday to Friday;
- readiness for occasional field trips (tree inventories, pre-design site inspections)
- own business activity (B2B cooperation)

**We offer:**

- a permanent cooperation agreement (contract) for a 3-month trial period, and then for one year or longer;
- cooperation with an experienced team, with an established market position (PAK IKROPKA has been operating since 2007) with access to interesting commissions, work under the supervision of experienced designers – landscape architects;
- workstation in our office – stationary work in the studio office in Wrocław (or hybrid – to be agreed);
- internal and external training;
- substantive support – especially at the beginning, to reduce any stress related to the new situation, workplace;
- remuneration: dependent on qualifications and experience, ranging from 6,000 PLN – 7,500 PLN gross / month under the form of employment – B2B cooperation – own business activity required.

**From when?**

We aim to start cooperation from the end of May – the last week of May we plan to dedicate to a one-week training and onboarding of the new collaborator in our office in Wrocław, and then from June to commence the execution of works.
