We have received references from the company Wrocławskim Centrum Treningowym SPARTAN spółką z ograniczoną odpowiedzialnością with its registered office in Wrocław for the conducted [dendrology supervision](/oferta/nadzory/) in a historic park in Wrocław – Park Południowy.


![We have received new references]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/aktualnosci-spartanikropka-kopia.jpg)

*References from Wrocławskie Centrum Treningowe SPARTAN*

Recently we have received references from the company Wrocławskim Centrum Treningowym SPARTAN spółką z ograniczoną odpowiedzialnością with its registered office in Wrocław for the conducted dendrology supervision in a historic park in Wrocław - Park Południowy.

Mgr inż. landscape architect Dominika Krop – Andrzejczuk – green space inspector supervision, dendrology supervision inspector, in the period 08.02.2023 to 08.08.2023 on the commission of the company Wrocławskim Centrum Treningowym SPARTAN spółką z ograniczoną odpowiedzialnością with its registered office in Wrocław provided services related to the conduct of supervision over greenery in the historic area in connection with the implementation of a construction investment on the premises of the historic Park Południowy in Wrocław – a park of 35 ha area in the monument register – for the task: "Construction of roofed tennis courts together with a tennis wall, site development and necessary technical infrastructure as well as demolition of part of the existing construction objects at ul. Pułtuska 13 in Wrocław".


The scope of activities related to the performance of the function of green space inspector / dendrology inspector by Ms Mgr inż. arch. landscape architect Dominika – Krop Andrzejczuk included in particular:

– Familiarization with the design documentation, the contract for the execution of the construction and the building permit.
– Control of compliance of the performed works with the technical design and the contract.
– Permanent presence on the construction site focused on supervision of the investment implementation process in the context of protection of the historic park tree stand, in accordance with the design documentation and the conservator's permit.
– Participation in Construction Boards.
– Reporting to the Ordering Party of the scope of performed activities in written form.
– Keeping photographic documentation from the construction.
– Proposing and issuing opinions on changes and supplements to the documentation in case of ascertaining irregularities in the scope of technology and usefulness of the documentation for the purposes of construction and future function of the object.
– Agreeing together with the construction management or author supervision on the necessity of performing substitute or additional works and proposing to the Ordering Party in order to obtain acceptance.
– Suspension of construction upon ascertaining irregularities in the implementation of the investment in the scope of environmental protection, nature protection until the irregularities are removed.
