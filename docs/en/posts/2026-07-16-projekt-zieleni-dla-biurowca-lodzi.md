Greenery surrounding an office and service building, greenery on the roof.

![GREEN SPACE DESIGN FOR AN OFFICE BUILDING IN ŁÓDŹ]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/news/biurowiec-lodz-ikropka.jpg)

We have commenced design work on the green space design concept within the site development of a newly emerging office and service building in Łódź. The green space design focuses mostly on the area of the underground garage roof slab.

The scope of IKROPKA's work includes the preliminary concept, design concept and construction project. The project covers the entirety of the areas surrounding the office building. The objective of the project is to design attractive, representative greenery.
