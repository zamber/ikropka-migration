Green space design and green roof systems design for office buildings


We are finalizing the construction project documentation for the green space design and green roof systems design for the site development of phase II of the Business Garden investment in Wrocław.

The project covers an area of several hectares of development around office buildings and is the next phase of construction of the Business Garden complex. The design is being developed in constant coordination with many disciplines – architects, road engineers, structural engineers and more. Working in such a team requires continuous coordination, good cooperation and great precision. We are glad to be part of it!
