After many months of intensive work on author supervision, we can present to you a completed project! Last Sunday, the official opening of Park Sikorskiego in Świdnica took place. The park underwent revitalization in accordance with our program-spatial concept and the construction project for greenery, as well as under our author supervision.

The work brought a wealth of experience and new challenges, and resulted in new relationships. We are very pleased with the completion and the good cooperation with the City of Świdnica, as well as with the positive opinions about the park reaching us! We are proud of this joint work.

The scope of the project tasks included: detailed tree inventory, tree stand management design, historical archive query, compositional layout, glade layout, view axes, pedestrian–cyclist communication layout, terrain relief, landscape furniture, lighting, dog run, agreements with the monument conservator, and others.

In 2018, we provided author supervision over the implementation of the investment.

More about the concept in our [portfolio](https://ikropka.eu/projekt/rewaloryzacja-parku-sikorskiego/)! Below we present our photos from the implementation and, note the novelty – a film!, also of our authorship. We invite you to watch.
