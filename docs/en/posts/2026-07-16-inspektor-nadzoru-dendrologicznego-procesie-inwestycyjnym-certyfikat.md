In 2023, mgr inż. Dominika Krop - Andrzejczuk - landscape architect, dendrologist / arborist, green space inspector supervision and dendrological / arboricultural supervision inspector completed a course and passed an exam obtaining the certificate and title of Dendrological Supervision Inspector in the investment process.

For over a dozen years we have provided [dendrological supervision](/en/services/nadzory/) and supervision over the execution of construction works near trees for the purpose of tree protection on the construction site as well as dendrological supervision during tree care and proper tree removal, also in areas under conservator protection, historic greenery, historic parks, etc. Dominika Krop – Andrzejczuk also performs the function of works manager in areas of historic greenery. In our team we strive to update knowledge and raise competences every few years in areas where knowledge evolves significantly and approaches to works on various forms of greenery, especially trees, change.

In 2023, mgr inż. Dominika Krop – Andrzejczuk – landscape architect, dendrologist / arborist, green space inspector supervision and dendrological / arboricultural supervision inspector completed a course and passed an exam obtaining the certificate and title of Dendrological Supervision Inspector in the investment process.

The course of Dendrological Supervision Inspector in the investment process comprised three training sessions – together 30 training hours.

I. Training I – Organization of construction works in wooded areas – 10 training hours. The training program covered the following topics:

– legal instruments protecting trees in the investment process.
– impact of investments on the survival chances of trees
– legal standards guaranteeing tree protection in the investment process
– technical solutions for tree protection on the construction site
– nature-based care treatments enabling tree protection during works
– assessment and valuation of tree damage resulting from construction works – total damage and partial damage

Development of the training program and substantive supervision – dr inż. Marzena Suchocka

The issue of tree protection in investment areas is very complex, as it requires analysis of several legal acts, from which we can derive the duties of authorities, the investor and possible administrative sanctions for non-compliance with legal provisions. The problem is finding specific regulations governing this matter, since the acts containing legal provisions relating to the investment process, both before its commencement and during its duration, do not refer directly to tree protection.

II. Training II – Design in the vicinity of trees – engineering methods for improving habitat conditions of urban trees – 10 training hours. The training program covered the following topics:

– green infrastructure and methods for increasing small retention. The role of vegetation in increasing small retention and resilience to climate change
– developmental requirements of trees in the context of investments, as well as engineering solutions and nature-based rehabilitation actions for proper tree protection
– overview of design techniques and technologies reducing the impact of infrastructure on trees.
– a representative of a design company will present an example street redevelopment project. Presentation of a tree-friendly alternative solution and its advantages.

Development of the training program and substantive supervision – dr inż. Marzena Suchocka

III. Training III – Tree stand management in the investment process – Tree Protection Project (POD) – 10 training hours

– tree care at various development phases
– design documents enabling tree protection
– criteria for qualifying trees for retention and removal
– pre-investment tree assessment in the context of their development chances after construction works are completed

Training conducted by: Krystyna Ładyżyńska-Adamowicz – Conservator for Historic Greenery
Development of the training program and substantive supervision – dr inż. Marzena Suchocka
