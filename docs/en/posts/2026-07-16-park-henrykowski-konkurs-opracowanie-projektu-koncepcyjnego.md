Designers from PAK IKROPKA, in cooperation with KMP Poniewierka sp. z o.o. and with AIS Śliwa Dobosz sp. z o.o. sp.k., participated in a single-stage competition for the development of a concept design for the site development of the post-railway area in the vicinity of Henrykowska and Paczkowska streets in Wrocław. The area will adopt the name "Park Henrykowski" in the future.

As part of the competition, the task of the designers was to incorporate the maximum number of community recommendations while preserving the original, industrial character of the place, which is a railway area.

The project team of PAK IKROPKA, KMP Poniewierka and AIS Śliwa Dobosz was guided, in the development of the design concept, by three fundamental guidelines for the project:

- creation of a wild urban park through the use of the abundance of existing vegetation and biological diversity, preservation of the landscape and natural values of the natural ecosystem, and actions aimed at supporting natural succession,
- creation of a place for relaxation and recreation, both active and passive, for local residents across a diverse age range,
- creation of new pedestrian and cycling connections, utilizing the natural terrain relief and leading them along the traces of former tracks, embankments and ravines, emphasizing the railway character of the park.
