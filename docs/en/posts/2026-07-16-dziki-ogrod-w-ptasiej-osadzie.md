A garden at a recreational center in the spirit of naturalistic, rustic, wild greenery.


A garden in an idyllic atmosphere for a newly emerging recreational center in Joachimówka near Milicz, whose character is to benefit from the charms and blessings of regional herbs, edible and cultivated plants, as well as medicinal ones, bringing traditional, wild cuisine closer to the guests.

We are in the process of implementing a commission for an extraordinary, idyllic garden full of wildness and rusticity. The garden will complement the newly emerging recreational center located in the beautiful regions of Milicz, within an area protected by as many as two different Natura 2000 sites and the Barycz Valley Landscape Park.

The garden layout is to reference the landscape of the surrounding rural areas and is to be maintained in a free, rustic-naturalistic style, inspired by traditional village flower-herb-vegetable gardens. For our Client, we are preparing a green space design, the selection of landscape furniture elements, color schemes and surface technologies, all tailored to the specific requirements and idea of the Place!

The functional program of the investment will be oriented toward bringing nature and its economic role closer to users, among others in cuisine, herbology and folk medicine, therefore in the greenery composition the focus was placed primarily on the utilitarian values of the selected plants.

The designed landscape furniture will fit into the rustic style of the place by its character, therefore we propose elements made of natural materials, such as wood and brushwood.
