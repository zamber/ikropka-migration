Less than a year after the commencement of construction works concerning the site development at the new residential estate of Archicom "River Point" on Kępa Mieszczańska in Wrocław, the effects of the first stage of works are already visible, concerning the surfaces, the parking lot integrated into the site development, the play and recreation zone, partially executed landscape furniture and lighting, as well as greenery.

The implementation of the investment has been ongoing since 2016. The implementation of the site development project started at the beginning of 2018.

The site development project, the green space design, and the landscape furniture design, as well as all pre-design studies in this scope, were created in our studio IKROPKA in the years 2016 – 2018 in cooperation with the designers of Archicom Studio.

For the Investor, we provide author supervision.

This is the first stage of implementation of this investment; we are awaiting the subsequent ones with impatience, which will allow for the cohesion of the whole.
