We have completed the design work on the concept for the revitalization of a large park in Świdnica! We carried out a tree inventory for a vast area, inventorying over 2 thousand trees! The resulting concept serves as the basis for obtaining a grant for the park's revitalization.


Behind us is work on the design of a historic park. Our scope of work included, among others, the preparation of a tree inventory (we inventoried over 2000 trees!), cooperation in the historical archival research with dr. I. Bińkowska, pre-design analyses (analyses of the communication layout, landscape interiors, connections and visual axes), as well as agreement of the concept with the Monument Conservator (Wojewódzki Urząd Ochrony Zabytków – delegation in Wałbrzych).

Among the inventoried trees were historical gems of rarely encountered varieties… More soon in our portfolio!
