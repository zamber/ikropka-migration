Lots of good changes for IKROPKA in the New Year! We have moved to a new office and have a new, refreshed logo. Work is in full swing!


![NOWY ROK, NOWY LOKAL I NOWE LOGO]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/nowy-rok.jpg)

We have completed the move! From now on, you can find us just a few numbers further, still in the vicinity of Sky Tower, and closer to the center, at our new address: ul. Powstańców Śląskich 48, 53-333 Wrocław.

The new office gives us new possibilities, a large space, more workstations, and it is a pleasure to work. We invite you to visit 🙂

And since we were making changes, we went all out! Apart from the address, our logo has also changed. The new design is the work of our favorite Lemonhills. The graphic mark has been maintained in a similar style of many dots, each of which received an individual appearance. An important element building the coherence of the new identity is the typeface selected to match the character of the entire logotype and the company's visual identity.
