We operate throughout Poland and … beyond!

We are greatly pleased that Clients reach out to us from all over Poland, but also from abroad! This year we signed our first international contract.

For our new Clients from Goerlitz, we are creating a garden in a beautiful space. The house is situated in a manor-like surroundings, adjacent to a historic palace, and from the garden there is a picturesque view of a hill with a historic observation tower. Additionally, in the garden there is a monumental common beech.
