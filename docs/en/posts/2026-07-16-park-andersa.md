The design concept of Andersa Park is considered in the context of the functional-spatial connections of the southern wedge. The park is part of the southern recreational route running from the Partynice-Ołtaszyn-Wojszyce housing estate to the city center and the Main Station. Along it are located sports centers significant for the whole city (Aquapark, CR Spiska), sports infrastructure (Pumptrack Black Wheel, Parkour Park), education centers (LO V, ZSG, ZS nr 2, Młodzieżowy Ośrodek Wychowawczy, Przedszkole nr 49) as well as health centers (Wojskowy Szpital Kliniczny, PAN, Przylądek Nadziei, Uniwersytecki Szpital Kliniczny).

Park Ansersa is part of the southern wedge of Wrocław, i.e. a sequence of green areas, which includes Wzgórze Andersa, Andersa Park, Skowroni Park, numerous allotment gardens and cemeteries. Each park has its own character, with which it is associated by the residents of Wrocław.

Andersa Park itself can be described as a "transitional" park. It borders the tram depot, LO V, Zespołem Szkół nr 2, Młodzieżowym Ośrodkiem Wychowawczym, a transformer station and a sports center on ul. Spiskiej. The appropriate communication layout should refer to local conditions.
