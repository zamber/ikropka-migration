With the Municipal Greenery Board in Wrocław we have signed three new Agreements for site development projects. Two of them are projects selected under the Wrocław Civic Budget 2018.

New design topics undertaken in our Studio are:

– Green Wojszyce. Site development project of the square at Kosmiczna, Chlebowa and Spokojna streets in Wrocław. – under WBO 2018

– Złotnicki Gródek for children. Site development project of a fragment of Złotnicki Park in Wrocław. – under WBO 2018

– Park at Wolbromska St. Site development project.

All projects will begin with detailed site inventory and tree inventory, which will provide information on existing trees and shrubs, their condition, girths and compositional values.

Subsequently, for each design topic two development concepts will be created for consultation with the Ordering Party and the WBO project Leader. Based on the selected concept, a building project will be prepared for arrangements with the relevant Offices. The final outcome will be a construction project together with cost estimates, bills of quantities and detailed technical specifications.
