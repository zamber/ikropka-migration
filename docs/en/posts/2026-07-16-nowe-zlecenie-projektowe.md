Site development project for an urban square with a flowering meadow in Wrocław.

We are pleased to announce that we have signed a new contract for the execution of a site development project for an urban square dedicated to the theme of bees in the city! The project entitled "BEES IN THE CITY – HONEY MEADOW AND HOTELS FOR POLLINATORS BETWEEN HERMANOWSKA AND CHOCIEBUSKA STREETS IN WROCŁAW" is a consequence of a winning idea in the Wrocław Participatory Budget.

A thematic flowering meadow is planned, which will be a foraging site for insects, and thanks to the installation of comfortable seating, it will also become an ideal place for rest and relaxation for residents. Furthermore, the project provides for the installation of approximately 20 houses for pollinators. Within our scope of design work is full, multi-discipline construction and construction project documentation for all elements of the site development.

We are sharing the beginnings of the concept works; behind us already is the tree inventory, study and site analyses, and dendrology management plan. We will show more soon!
