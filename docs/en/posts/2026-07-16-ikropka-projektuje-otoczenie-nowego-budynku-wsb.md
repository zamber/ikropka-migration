IKROPKA has established cooperation regarding the site development of the surroundings of the new building of Wrocławska Szkoła Bankowej.

The space at the new WSB headquarters is to meet the high standards of modern landscape architecture. Inspirations for the first ideas were developed in cooperation with architects who emphasize uniqueness and diversification of terrain levels. The site development and greenery will be created in reference to the architectural forms of the building.

Variant design concepts will be prepared, on the basis of which we will develop the construction project.
