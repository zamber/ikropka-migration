We design flowering meadows in the city and flower Wrocław!

In the IKROPKA project for Plac Powstańców Śląskich in Wrocław, colloquially called the "Rondo", alongside colourful perennial compositions, we decided to design flowering meadows! One of the selected mixes is "Smogówka" – a meadow with anti-smog properties. Beyond aesthetic values, we emphasize environmental benefits.


The main objective of the project carried out on behalf of Zarząd Zieleni Miejskiej in Wrocław, under the Wrocławskiego Budżetu Obywatelskiego 2018, is **flowering** of the roundabout. Due to the historic character of the entire urban layout of the roundabout, in our design we also considered adapting the design solutions to the historical context of the place.

Since the roundabout is densely planted with tall tree stands and numerous shrubs, the project focused on new bed compositions in the perennial style. To this idea we decided to add flowering meadows, which will perfectly fit the new, decorative and colourful character of the greenery.

A curiosity is the *anti-smog meadow* applied in the project. The mix was developed as a result of several years of research on the absorption of dust forming smog by meadow plants, conducted by specialists from Szkoła Główna Gospodarstwa Wiejskiego in Warszawa. The so-called "Smogówka" has already been tested in Kraków, now it is Wrocław's turn! It will delight the eyes of tram passengers passing through the roundabout and locally improve climatic conditions.
