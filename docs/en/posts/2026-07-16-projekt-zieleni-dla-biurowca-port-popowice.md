In Wrocław, on the site of the former river port, a modern, pro-ecological residential development is being created – Port Popowice. It is here that IKROPKA will design the greenery around the office building.


The development project references the industrial past of the site. An important aspect of the construction of the office building are the requirements of the LEED GOLD certificate, defined by the Polish Green Building Council, which assume the use of pro-ecological technologies. The green space design prepared by our Studio in cooperation with the renowned architectural studio WXCA will fit into this character and specificity through the use of plant species with appropriate requirements while offering high aesthetic values. A preliminary design concept for the greenery has already been developed.
