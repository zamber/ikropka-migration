Park Uśmiechu was named the best public space in Poland completed in 2020.

This award was won in the Polski Krajobraz 2020 XXL plebiscite - in the public space category. The most valuable vote for us is from the audience, meaning it is the opinion of the users themselves. The Park Uśmiechu investment - a square at ul. Wolbromska in Wrocław - is the result of cooperation between the PAK IKROPKA team with the Residents, the Investor - Zarząd Zieleni Miejskiej, and the Contractor Tribud. We thank you for the fruitful cooperation.
