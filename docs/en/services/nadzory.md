## Green Space Project Implementation Supervision

We offer **author and investor supervision** for the implementation of site development projects, green space designs and plantings.

### Types of Supervision

**Author Supervision**
- Compliance control with the design
- Consultations with the contractor
- Introducing design changes
- Work acceptance procedures

**Investor Supervision**
- Representing the investor on site
- Quality control of materials and workmanship
- Work settlement (measurements, construction log)
- Coordination with other trades

**Environmental/Dendrological Supervision**
- Tree protection during construction
- Compliance control with tree protection plan
- Ongoing maintenance recommendations
- Photographic documentation

### Supervision Scope

- **Site Inspections** - site visits according to schedule
- **Documentation** - supervision log, protocols, notes
- **Coordination** - consultations with contractor, designers of other trades
- **Acceptance** - partial and final work acceptance
- **Settlement** - measurements, as-built cost estimates

### For Which Projects?

- Parks and green areas
- Urban greenery (streets, squares, roundabouts)
- Private and residential gardens
- Revitalization of historic monuments
- Development projects

### Benefits of Supervision

✅ **Compliance with Design** - investment looks like in the visualization
✅ **Execution Quality** - control of materials, plantings, infrastructure
✅ **Time Savings** - quick problem solving on site
✅ **Cost Savings** - avoiding errors and rework
✅ **Warranty** - proper plant acceptance, acceptance documentation
