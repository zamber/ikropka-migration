## Urban and Residential Greenery Design

We design **urban and residential greenery** - tree and shrub plantings, flower beds, flowering meadows, infrastructure-accompanying greenery.

### Design Scope

- **Urban greenery** - streets, squares, roundabouts, plazas
- **Residential greenery** - common areas, playgrounds, pedestrian routes
- **Flowering meadows** - biodiversity, low maintenance costs
- **Beds and plant compositions** - perennial and seasonal
- **Greenery in public space** - parking lots, kindergarten yards

### What Do We Deliver?

- **Concept design** - 2D/3D visualizations
- **Technical design** - construction drawings, estimates
- **Plant species list** with requirements
- **First-year care instructions**
- **Investment cost estimate**
- **Implementation supervision** (optional)

### For Whom?

- Municipalities and local governments
- Developers
- Housing communities and cooperatives
- Commercial property managers
- Companies and institutions

### Design Principles

- **Native species** - climate adaptation
- **Low maintenance costs** - resistant plants, flowering meadows
- **Biodiversity** - insect-friendly vegetation
- **Year-round aesthetics** - species selection with different blooming periods
