## Emergency Dendrology Service

We offer **24/7 dendrology assistance** - rapid response in emergency situations related to trees.

### When Do You Need Emergency Service?

- **After storms and windstorms** - assessment of damaged trees
- **Safety hazard** - tree over house, road, power lines
- **Tree failure** - broken branches, fallen tree
- **Urgent assessment** - need for quick decision about tree fate
- **Neighbor conflict** - mediation, independent opinion

### What Do We Offer?

- **Emergency call-out** (within 24h)
- **On-site tree condition assessment**
- **Verbal opinion** or written dendrology expert assessment
- **Immediate recommendations** - securing, reduction, removal
- **Contact with care companies** (if needed)

### Service Area

Wrocław and surroundings (up to 50 km).
In justified cases - all of Poland.
