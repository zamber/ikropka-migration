We prepare site development projects for public, private, commercial, residential, recreational, and historic areas, as well as areas under monument protection. We take on every challenge and invite you to cooperate!

## Site Development Projects

We prepare concept, construction, and execution site development projects (PZT). We design with passion and experience. We manage space creatively to meet the Client's expectations, and we prepare our studies reliably and on time. A site development project prepared by experienced landscape architects trained in designing spaces that meet the needs of their users and all technical requirements, with modern solutions in spatial projects.

Thanks to the knowledge and experience gained at all stages of investment, including supervision work on the construction site, we know how to design to avoid additional costs and unnecessary problems at later stages of investment implementation!

We design spaces on green roofs! Space above an underground parking lot can serve as an attractive residential area for residents. Well-designed greenery and space will increase the value of any residential, office, and other development investment!

We approach every project individually, and the final composition of the site development project (PZT) documentation depends on the complexity of the subject and the Ordering Party's requirements. The resulting studies are interdisciplinary site development projects covering multilevel solutions of various aspects.

A special field of site development projects are projects in areas with mature trees or valuable old-growth woodland. We use dendrological knowledge there and design solutions around trees using non-invasive, modern design technologies and construction methods, thanks to which damage can be avoided and trees can be ensured to survive in good sanitary condition.

We prepare site development projects using AutoCAD software, we cooperate with Architects and all necessary branch specialists, and we provide a full Team of designers necessary to prepare the site development project for all agreements. We conduct administrative procedures, obtain opinions and agreements, building permits, and construction notifications.

When designing on a designated plot, we always consider landscape and spatial connections and strive for the best possible quality! When designing in the surroundings of buildings, we cooperate with interior architects and architects, analyzing the style of buildings, facades, and interior decor, and the type of materials used. Thanks to this, we achieve compositional and material coherence of the exterior and interior, and the surroundings become a functional extension of the building. The design stage is preceded by numerous pre-design analyses, e.g. of natural resources including tree stands (tree inventory), traffic, spatial composition, spatial and landscape connections, view, historical, etc.

We also prepare site development projects for historic subjects. We create revitalization and revitalization projects. Design work is preceded by thorough historical analyses and research. In this field we have experience and have been active for 15 years already! We also recommend our other services such as green space designs and landscape furniture element designs. Thanks to our interdisciplinary team, we provide fast and comprehensive service for every investment!
