We prepare designs and study documents for historic monuments, i.e. those under conservator protection, entered in the register or inventory of monuments. We invite you to cooperate!

## Revitalization project of a historic park

We are experts in preparing comprehensive documents concerning historic greenery layouts – we will perform a historic park design or a revitalization project of a historic garden, square or green area fully professionally.

Our services include reconstruction designs, revitalization and revitalization of greenery and historic green areas under conservator supervision. We offer a wide range of services, starting from field inventories, through analyses to detailed design documents.

We prepare complete design documentation, starting from the functional-spatial concept, through multidisciplinary construction and construction projects in the scope of landscape architecture, greenery, communication and water system, landscape furniture and lighting.

We prepare documentation necessary and required by monument protection offices. For these needs we prepare study documents concerning greenery – tree inventories, tree stand management designs, dendrology opinions and expert assessments, as well as dendrochronological analyses – age analyses of tree stands allowing to distinguish trees of exceptional values, particularly important from the point of view of park composition, or species analyses of tree stands allowing to learn the dynamics of changes in the dendroflora of the layout. In the context of the composition of the whole layout we prepare analyses of compositional-landscape and functional-spatial transformations, as well as assessments of the degree of preservation of historic monuments and the historic layout, and archival queries with conservator conclusions of an art historian.

In the case of preparing a tree stand management design for the dendrology management plan for trees growing in historic areas, pursuant to Art. 37a of the Act of 23 July 2003 on monument protection and care of monuments (Journal of Laws 2022.0.840), the works and documentation may be carried out by a person who "completed second-cycle studies or uniform master's studies in the scope of conservation and restoration of works of art or monument conservation and who, after commencing second-cycle studies or after passing the sixth semester of uniform master's studies, for at least 9 months participated in conservator works, restoration works or conservator research conducted for monuments entered in the register, the inventory of a museum being a cultural institution or classified to one of the categories referred to in Art. 14a List of Heritage Treasures § 2." These requirements are met, among others, by Pracownia Architektury Krajobrazu IKROPKA Dominika Krop – Andrzejczuk, operating throughout Poland.

Our documents allow to reliably reconstruct the former park composition – i.e. the course of paths, the form of water systems, gardens, landscape furniture and to supplement the tree stand with verified, historic species of trees and shrubs. Depending on the needs, our documents may also serve to prepare urban planning guidelines.

Field inventories, depending on the scope of the task, cover various elements. This may be the tree stand as well as other forms of greenery – ground flora, garden parterres, shrubs, gardens, planters and elements important for the composition of the layout, e.g. garden structures, landscape furniture, communication system, water system or terrain relief.

In revitalization projects we adapt the layouts to contemporary realities taking into account the needs of users. We introduce new functions referring to elements of the historic composition, while using knowledge of modern technologies and world standards in designing green public spaces.

We are able to conduct the whole process of consultations and formal-legal arrangements in offices (conservator permits, building permits) necessary to commence the investment.
