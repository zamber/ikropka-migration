## Tree Safety Inspections

We offer **regular tree inspections** for hazards in urban areas - parks, squares, residential areas, industrial sites.

### Service Scope

- **Visual health assessment** of trees
- **Structural assessment** - stability and anchoring check
- **Hazard identification** - dead branches, diseases, cavities
- **Maintenance recommendations** - sanitary cuts, reductions, removals
- **Photographic and descriptive documentation**
- **Schedule for next inspections**

### For Whom?

- Property managers
- Housing communities and cooperatives
- Municipalities and local governments
- Park and green area managers
- Companies and institutions

### Inspection Frequency

According to **PN-B-12080:1997** standard, recommended:
- **Basic inspection** - once a year (spring/autumn)
- **Detailed inspection** - every 3-5 years
- **Emergency inspection** - after extreme weather events

### Documentation Format

- Inspection report (PDF)
- Tree layout map
- Recommendations with implementation deadlines
- Hazard classification (urgent/medium-term/long-term)
